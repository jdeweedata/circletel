# Split Face Typography Portrait

> Dramatic split composition with text masking effect

## Pattern Overview

Creates high-contrast B&W portraits where half the face is revealed through letter shapes. Perfect for motivational messaging and personal branding.

## Template

```
High-contrast black and white split composition portrait,
vertical split at nose bridge, left half showing realistic
photographic face of [subject description], right half pure
white background with large serif typography "[TEXT]" where
letters reveal face through text shapes, dramatic chiaroscuro
lighting with hard side lighting, heavy slab serif uppercase
letters stacked vertically, minimal composition, editorial
portrait photography, strong shadows, photorealistic skin
texture, no AI-generated text on face side, 4K, 9:16
```

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| `[subject description]` | Person characteristics | "confident South African businessman in his 40s" |
| `[TEXT]` | Typography text (3-6 letters work best) | "LEAD", "GROW", "WIN" |

## Key Elements

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `vertical split at nose bridge` | Creates dramatic asymmetry |
| `chiaroscuro lighting` | High contrast, dramatic mood |
| `slab serif uppercase stacked` | Bold, authoritative typography |
| `face revealed through letters` | Creates memorable visual tension |
| `B&W` | Timeless, editorial quality |

## Typography Tips

- **Best letters**: 4-6 characters work best (LEAD, GROW, RISE)
- **Avoid**: Full words like "COMMUNICATE" - too many letters
- **Stack**: Letters vertically for stronger impact
- **Font style**: Heavy slab serif > thin sans-serif
