# Flash Photography Editorial Portrait

> 90s-inspired B&W flash photography with raw authenticity

## Pattern Overview

Creates hyper-realistic black and white portraits with direct flash, capturing a vintage 90s editorial aesthetic. Perfect for authentic "real people" marketing and technician team photos.

## Template

```
Hyper-realistic 4K black and white portrait photography,
direct flash photography creating vintage 90s editorial
aesthetic, [subject description], hard shadows against
plain [background color] background, ultra-detailed skin
texture with fine film grain, sharp focus on subject,
candid [mood] expression, natural imperfections visible,
high contrast with bright flash highlights, authentic
documentary style, magazine editorial quality, 85mm lens,
no AI-generated text, 4K, [aspect ratio]
```

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| `[subject description]` | Person and attire | "South African technician in work uniform" |
| `[background color]` | Simple backdrop | "white", "light gray", "pale blue" |
| `[mood]` | Expression type | "confident", "focused", "friendly", "determined" |
| `[aspect ratio]` | Output format | "4:5", "1:1", "3:4" |

## Key Elements

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `direct flash` | Creates signature hard shadows |
| `90s editorial` | Vintage authentic feel |
| `ultra-detailed skin texture` | Realistic, not airbrushed |
| `natural imperfections` | Human authenticity |
| `fine film grain` | Analog quality |
| `plain background` | Focus on subject |

## Flash Photography Characteristics

- **Hard shadows**: No soft diffusion
- **Bright highlights**: On forehead, nose, cheekbones
- **Sharp falloff**: Background slightly darker
- **Red-eye potential**: Adds authenticity (optional)
- **Flat lighting**: Direct flash removes dimension

2. Ensure contrast is high (flash aesthetic)
3. Add subtle grain if needed
4. Add CircleTel logo watermark
5. Optional: Add single orange (#F5831F) element (name tag, logo on shirt)

## Authenticity Tips

- **Don't over-retouch**: The style celebrates imperfection
- **Natural expressions**: Not overly posed or smiling
- **Work attire**: Real uniforms and equipment
- **Diverse representation**: Reflects South African demographics
- **Character**: Lines, freckles, scars add authenticity
