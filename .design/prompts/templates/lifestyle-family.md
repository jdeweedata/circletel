## Variables to Customize

| Placeholder | Options |
|-------------|---------|
| `[family demographic]` | diverse family, multigenerational, young family |
| `[home setting]` | living room, home office, bedroom, kitchen |
| `[family activity]` | streaming, video calls, gaming, homework |
| `[connectivity cues]` | WiFi icons, smooth playback, video quality |
| `[African-inspired elements]` | art, textiles, decor, patterns |
| `[publication style]` | National Geographic, editorial, lifestyle |

## Tips

1. **Diverse representation** - South Africa is diverse, show it
2. **Modern settings** - avoid dated furniture or technology
3. **African elements** - subtle cultural touches, not stereotypes
4. **Natural lighting** - warm, inviting, not studio-perfect
5. **Devices should work** - show stable connections, not buffering
6. **Authentic moments** - candid, not posed
7. **Router visible** - subtly show CircleTel hardware when possible
