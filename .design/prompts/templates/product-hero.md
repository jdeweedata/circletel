# Product Hero Angle Template

> Dramatic 3/4 angle shots for marketing and landing pages

## Pattern

```

visible, [accent elements], placed on [surface], [background style],
cinematic lighting from [direction], [lighting effects], depth of
field with sharp product focus, commercial advertising photography,
85mm lens, f/2.8, [resolution], [aspect_ratio]
```

## Variables to Customize

| Placeholder | Options |
|-------------|---------|
| `[signature feature]` | LED indicators, antennas, ports |
| `[accent elements]` | cables, glow, reflections |
| `[surface]` | floating, wooden, glass, metal |
| `[background style]` | gradient, lifestyle, studio |
| `[direction]` | top-left, top-right, above |
| `[lighting effects]` | lens flare, rim light, glow |

## Tips

1. **3/4 angle** (45°) shows depth and multiple product faces
2. **Orange LED glow** creates brand recognition
3. **Dark backgrounds** make products pop dramatically
4. **Shallow depth of field** (f/2.8) creates premium feel
5. **Reflection surfaces** add sophistication
6. **Rim lighting** separates product from background
