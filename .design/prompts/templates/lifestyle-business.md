## Variables to Customize

| Placeholder | Options |
|-------------|---------|
| `[business setting]` | conference room, open office, private office |
| `[professional activity]` | video call, collaboration, presentation |
| `[technology setup]` | video conferencing, multi-monitor, mobile |
| `[professional demographics]` | executive, team, entrepreneur |
| `[lighting style]` | natural, professional, warm |
| `[depth of field]` | f/2.8 shallow, f/4 moderate, f/8 deep |

## Tips

1. **Diverse professionals** - reflect South Africa's business demographics
2. **Modern offices** - show contemporary African business settings
3. **Visible technology** - laptops, displays, but not dated equipment
4. **Video call quality** - no buffering or pixelation shown
5. **Local context** - Johannesburg skylines, local businesses
6. **Router placement** - subtle CircleTel hardware in scene
7. **Professional but warm** - corporate yet approachable
