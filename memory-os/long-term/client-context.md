## Competitive Position

- **Core moat:** 7,438 products across 5 distributors (Scoop, MiRO, Nology, Rectron, Apple iStore). No ISP competitor has hardware supply capability.
- **Competitors:** WebAfrica (consumer, Netflix #1), Afrihost (awards, 75K reviews), Vox (VoIP leader, 24/7 support, OpenSignal #1)
- **Uncontested space:** Managed IT + hardware supply + connectivity bundles. CircleTel is the only provider offering all three.
- **Speed moat:** SkyFibre Business deploys in 3-5 days vs. 6 weeks for traditional business fibre.
