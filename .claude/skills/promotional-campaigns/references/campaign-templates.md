# Campaign Templates

Ready-to-use templates for common promotional campaigns. Fill in the brackets with your specific details.

---

## Template 1: Product Launch Campaign

Use when launching a new product or major feature update.

```markdown
## [Product Name] Launch Campaign

**Type:** Product Launch
**Campaign Period:** [Start Date] - [End Date] ([X] weeks)
**Target Segment:** [Specific persona]
**Launch Product:** [Product name with SKU]

---

### Launch Offer

**The Deal:**
- [Primary offer: e.g., "First 50 customers get free installation"]
- [Secondary benefit: e.g., "Plus free router upgrade"]
- [Urgency element: e.g., "Offer ends [date] or when slots fill"]

**Pricing:**
| Package | Standard Price | Launch Price | Savings |
|---------|---------------|--------------|---------|
| [Package 1] | R[X]/mo | R[X]/mo | R[X] |
| [Package 2] | R[X]/mo | R[X]/mo | R[X] |

---

### Key Messages

**Headline:**
"[Action verb] [benefit]. [Secondary hook]."

**Subheadline:**
"[Expand on headline with specificity]"

**Value Propositions (3-4):**
1. [Technology/feature advantage]
2. [Business outcome benefit]
3. [Service/support differentiator]
4. [Price/value proposition]

**CTA:**
"[Action verb] + [What they get]"
E.g., "Check My Coverage", "Get My Quote", "Book Installation"

---

### Channel Plan

| Channel | Content | Timing |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Website | Landing page + banner | Launch day |
| Email | Announcement to list | Launch day |
| Social | Teaser → Launch → Reminder | -3d, 0, +7d |
| Sales | Outbound to target accounts | Launch +1d |
| Partners | Partner notification | Launch -1d |

---

### Asset Checklist

- [ ] Landing page copy (use copywriting skill)
- [ ] Email sequence (3 emails: announce, details, last chance)
- [ ] Social media posts (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram)
- [ ] Sales one-pager (PDF)
- [ ] AI image prompts (use brand-design skill)
- [ ] Demo video script (if applicable)

---

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| New sign-ups | [X] customers | CRM tracking |
| MRR generated | R[X] | Billing system |
| Landing page conversion | [X]% | Analytics |
| CAC for campaign | <R[X] | Total spend / customers |

---

### Margin Validation

**Product:** [Name]
**Launch effective price:** R[X]/mo
**COS floor:** R[X]
**Gross margin:** [X]% [✓ if ≥25%]

**Approved by:** [Name/Role] on [Date]
```

## Template 2: Seasonal Promotion

Use for Black Friday, tax season, back to school, and other calendar-driven campaigns.

```markdown
## [Season/Event] Promotion [Year]

**Type:** Seasonal
**Campaign Period:** [Start Date] - [End Date]
**Products Included:** [List products]

---

### The Offer

**Hero Deal:**
[Primary promotional offer with clear value]

**How It Works:**
1. [Step 1: Customer action]
2. [Step 2: What happens]
3. [Step 3: What they get]

**Terms:**
- Valid for new customers only / existing customers / all
- Minimum [X]-month commitment required
- Cannot be combined with other offers
- Subject to coverage check

---

### Campaign Theme

**Tagline:** "[Seasonal hook + brand connection]"

**Visual Direction:**
- Colors: [Seasonal colors + brand orange]
- Imagery: [Description for brand-design skill]
- Mood: [Energetic/Urgent/Celebratory/etc.]

---

### Channel Execution

**Week 1: Teaser**
- Social teaser posts (build anticipation)
- Email: "Something big is coming..."
- Website: Countdown banner

**Week 2-3: Main Push**
- Full campaign launch across all channels
- Daily social posts with offer details
- Email: Main announcement + benefits
- Paid social (if budget allows)

**Final Days: Urgency**
- "Last X days" messaging
- Email: Final reminder
- Social: Countdown posts

---

### Landing Page Structure

1. **Hero:** Big headline + offer + CTA
2. **Social proof:** Customer logos/testimonials
3. **Offer details:** Clear breakdown of what's included
4. **Product comparison:** Help choose right package
5. **FAQ:** Answer common questions
6. **Final CTA:** Repeat offer with urgency

---

### Messaging by Product

| Product | Seasonal Angle | Key Benefit |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| [Product 1] | [Relevant seasonal hook] | [Benefit] |
| [Product 2] | [Relevant seasonal hook] | [Benefit] |
| [Product 3] | [Relevant seasonal hook] | [Benefit] |

---

### Budget Allocation (if applicable)

| Item | Budget | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Paid social | R[X] | Focus on [platforms] |
| Design/assets | R[X] | [Description] |
| Installation subsidy | R[X] × [N] customers | If offering free install |
| Total | R[X] | |

---

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Over-subscription | Cap at [X] customers |
| Support overload | Pre-schedule extra coverage |
| Installation backlog | Pre-book installer capacity |
| Margin erosion | Daily margin monitoring |
```

## Template 3: Competitor Response Campaign

Use when reacting to a competitor's campaign or market move.

```markdown
## Counter-Campaign: [Competitor Name] Response

**Type:** Competitive Response
**Trigger:** [Competitor action that triggered this]
**Response Period:** [Start] - [End]
**Urgency:** [High/Medium/Low]

---

### Competitor Analysis

**What They Did:**
[Clear description of competitor's offer/campaign]

**Their Target:**
[Who they're trying to attract]

**Threat Assessment:**
- Overlap with our customers: [High/Medium/Low]
- Threat to pipeline: [High/Medium/Low]
- Requires response: [Yes/No]

---

### Our Strategy: [Differentiate/Value-Add/Ignore/Match]

**Rationale:**
[Why this strategy vs. alternatives]

**We Will NOT:**
- [What we won't do, e.g., "Match their price point"]
- [Price cuts that violate margin rules]

---

### Counter-Positioning

**Our Lead Message:**
"[Single powerful statement that positions us vs. competitor]"

**Comparison Points:**
| Their Claim | Reality | CircleTel Advantage |
|-------------|---------|---------------------|
| [Claim 1] | [Reality check] | [Our strength] |
| [Claim 2] | [Reality check] | [Our strength] |
| [Claim 3] | [Reality check] | [Our strength] |

---

### CircleTel Counter-Offer

**Product:** [Which product to promote]
**Offer:** [What we're offering in response]

**Value-adds (not price cuts):**
- [Value-add 1]
- [Value-add 2]

**Margin Validation:**
- Effective price: R[X]/mo
- COS: R[X]
- Gross margin: [X]% ✓

---

### Sales Team Enablement

**30-Second Pitch:**
"[Concise competitive pitch for sales team]"

**Objection Handling:**

| Customer Says | We Say |
|---------------|--------|
| "But [competitor] is cheaper" | "[Response focusing on value, not price]" |
| "I've heard good things about [competitor]" | "[Acknowledge, then differentiate]" |
| "Can you match their offer?" | "[Value-focused response]" |

**Proactive Outreach List:**
- [Customers at risk of switching]
- [Prospects who mentioned competitor]

---

### Timeline

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | Internal briefing, sales enablement |
| Day 2 | Counter-campaign live |
| Day 3-7 | Proactive outreach to at-risk accounts |
| Day 14 | Campaign effectiveness review |

---

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Retention rate during campaign | ≥[X]% |
| Competitive wins | [X] customers |
| Lost to competitor | <[X] customers |
```

## Template 4: Upsell/Cross-sell Campaign

Use to increase ARPU from existing customers.

```markdown
## Upsell Campaign: [Target Upgrade]

**Type:** Upsell
**Campaign Period:** [Start] - [End]
**Target Base:** [Which existing customers]

---

### Upsell Opportunity

**From:** [Current product/package]
**To:** [Target product/package]
**ARPU Increase:** R[X]/mo per customer
**Target Conversions:** [X] customers = R[X] incremental MRR

---

### Customer Trigger

Customers who:
- [ ] Have been active for ≥[X] months
- [ ] Usage is >80% of current allocation
- [ ] Haven't upgraded in [X] months
- [ ] Have [specific behavior indicating readiness]

---

### The Offer

**Why Upgrade Now:**
[Compelling reason tied to customer benefit]

**Limited-Time Incentive:**
- [Incentive: e.g., "First 3 months at current price"]
- Valid until: [Date]

**Upgrade Pricing:**
| Current | Upgrade To | Standard | Promo Price | Savings |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------------|---------|
| [Plan A] | [Plan B] | R[X]/mo | R[X]/mo | R[X] |

---

### Communication Sequence

**Email 1: Value-Focused (Day 1)**
Subject: "[Name], you're outgrowing your plan"
- Highlight usage patterns
- Show upgrade benefits
- Soft CTA

**Email 2: Offer Introduction (Day 5)**
Subject: "Upgrade offer inside"
- Present limited-time offer
- Clear comparison
- Strong CTA

**Email 3: Urgency (Day 12)**
Subject: "Offer expires [Day]"
- Final reminder
- Urgency messaging
- Direct CTA

---

### In-App/Dashboard Messaging (if applicable)

**Banner:**
"You're using [X]% of your speed. Upgrade now for R[X] more/mo."

**Usage Alert:**
"Your team could be faster. See upgrade options →"

---

### Sales Touchpoint

For high-value accounts:
- Account manager personal outreach
- Custom upgrade proposal if needed
- White-glove upgrade support
```

## Template 5: Win-back Campaign

Use to reactivate churned customers.

```markdown
## Win-Back Campaign: [Cohort Name]

**Type:** Win-back
**Campaign Period:** [Start] - [End]
**Target:** Customers churned [X]-[Y] months ago

---

### Target Criteria

Include customers who:
- Churned [X] to [Y] months ago
- Churn reason: [Specific reasons to target]
- Previous LTV: ≥R[X]
- Exclude: [Who NOT to target]

Estimated pool: [X] customers

---

### Win-Back Offer

**Acknowledgment:**
"[Honest acknowledgment of why they might have left]"

**What's Changed:**
- [Improvement 1 since they left]
- [Improvement 2]
- [Improvement 3]

**The Offer:**
- [Welcome back incentive: e.g., "Rejoin at your original rate"]
- [Loyalty bonus: e.g., "Plus R500 credit"]
- Valid for: [Timeframe]

---

### Communication Sequence

**Email 1: We Miss You (Day 1)**
Subject: "We've been thinking about you"
- Warm, personal tone
- No hard sell
- Survey link: "What would bring you back?"

**Email 2: What's New (Day 7)**
Subject: "Things have changed at CircleTel"
- Highlight improvements
- Customer success stories
- Soft mention of offer

**Email 3: The Offer (Day 14)**
Subject: "A special offer just for you"
- Clear win-back offer
- Limited time
- Easy reactivation CTA

**Email 4: Final Chance (Day 28)**
Subject: "Last chance: Your offer expires tomorrow"
- Urgency
- Recap offer
- One-click reactivation

---

### Handling Objections

| Reason They Left | Our Response |
|------------------|--------------|
| Price | "We now have more flexible packages starting at R[X]" |
| Service issues | "We've invested in [specific improvements]" |
| Moved locations | "We now cover [new areas]. Let's check your address." |
| Switched to competitor | "What would it take to win you back?" |

---

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | ≥[X]% |
| Response rate | ≥[X]% |
| Reactivation rate | ≥[X]% |
| Recovered MRR | R[X] |
```

## Template 6: Referral Bonus Campaign

Use to incentivize customer referrals.

```markdown
## Referral Campaign: [Campaign Name]

**Type:** Referral
**Campaign Period:** [Ongoing/Limited time]
**Budget:** R[X] per successful referral

---

### Referral Mechanics

**Referrer Gets:**
- [Primary reward: e.g., "R500 account credit"]
- [Bonus for multiple referrals: e.g., "R750 for 3+ referrals"]
- Applied: [When: after referee's first bill paid]

**Referee Gets:**
- [Welcome offer: e.g., "50% off first month"]
- [Or: "Free installation"]

**Terms:**
- Both parties must be CircleTel customers for ≥30 days
- Referee must sign up for [minimum package/commitment]
- No limit on referrals / Limited to [X] per customer
- Cash equivalent not available

---

### How to Refer

1. Log into CircleTel dashboard
2. Click "Refer a Friend"
3. Share unique referral link or enter friend's email
4. Get notified when reward is earned

---

### Communication to Existing Customers

**Email:**
Subject: "Get R[X] for every business you refer"

**Dashboard Banner:**
"Know a business that needs better connectivity? Refer them and earn R[X]"

**Invoice Footer:**
"Refer a friend, get R[X] credit. Details: circletel.co.za/refer"

---

### Target Advocates

Focus referral messaging on:
- Customers with NPS ≥9
- Long-tenure customers (12+ months)
- Customers who've given positive reviews
- High-engagement customers

---

### Tracking & Attribution

**Unique referral codes:** [Format: CUSTNAME-XXXX]
**Tracking system:** [CRM/Billing system]
**Attribution window:** [X] days from link click

---

### Campaign Boosts

**Limited-Time Double Rewards:**
- Period: [Dates]
- Message: "Double referral rewards this week only!"

**Referral Milestones:**
| Referrals | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| 3 | R250 extra bonus |
| 5 | R500 extra + CircleTel merch |
| 10 | R1,000 + Premium for 1 month |
```
