## Commission Structure

### MTN Tiers (CircleTel receives 30%)

| Monthly Subscription | MTN Rate | CircleTel Share |
|---------------------|----------|-----------------|
| R0-R99.99 | 4.75% | 1.425% |
| R100-R199.99 | 5.75% | 1.725% |
| R200-R299.99 | 7.25% | 2.175% |
| R300-R499.99 | 8.75% | 2.625% |
| R500-R999.99 | 9.75% | 2.925% |
| R1,000-R1,999.99 | 11.75% | 3.525% |
| R2,000+ | 13.75% | 4.125% |

### Commission Calculation

```
Total Contract Value = Monthly Subscription x Contract Term (months)
MTN Commission = Total Contract Value x MTN Commission Rate
CircleTel Commission = MTN Commission x 30%
```

### Example

**R999/month voice contract (24 months):**
- Contract Value: R999 x 24 = R23,976
- MTN Commission: R23,976 x 9.75% = R2,338
- CircleTel Share: R2,338 x 30% = R701
