## Quick Calculations

### Margin Calculator

```
Contribution Margin (R) = Retail Price - All-in Cost
Margin (%) = (Contribution / Retail) × 100
```

### Pro-Rata Calculator

```
Pro-rata = (MRC / Days in Month) × Days Remaining
```

### LTV Calculator

```
LTV = ARPU × Average Lifetime (months)
```
