## New Pricing Structure

### Tier Pricing & User Limits

| Tier | Monthly Price | Included M365 Licences | M365 Type | Add-on Rate |
|------|---------------|------------------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Essential** | R2,999 | 5 users | Business Basic | R179/user |
| **Professional** | R5,999 | 10 users | Business Standard | R329/user |
| **Premium** | R12,999 | 15 users | Business Premium | R549/user |
| **Enterprise** | POA (min R35k) | 20 users | E3 | R799/user |

### What This Means for Sales

1. **Essential tier** — Unchanged. Good entry point for SOHO/startups.

2. **Professional tier** — Price unchanged BUT now explicitly includes 10 M365 Business Standard licences. Customers with 11–25 users pay R329/user extra for additional users.
   - Example: 18-user business = R5,999 + (8 × R329) = **R8,631/mo**

3. **Premium tier** — Price increased from R9,999 to R12,999. Includes 15 M365 Business Premium licences. Additional users at R549/user.
   - Example: 35-user business = R12,999 + (20 × R549) = **R23,979/mo**

4. **Enterprise tier** — Now POA with minimum R35,000. Includes 20 M365 E3 licences. Always require custom scoping.

## Sales Objection Handling

### "Why did the Premium price go up?"

**Response:**
"We've restructured our pricing to be more transparent. The previous R9,999 price point was loss-making when we actually delivered all the services. The new R12,999 price includes 15 Microsoft 365 Business Premium licences (worth R549 each retail = R8,235 value), 200Mbps connectivity with automatic LTE backup, 24/7 support, and full security suite. You're still saving 30-40% versus buying these separately."

### "Can I get the old R9,999 price?"

**Response:**
"We can offer R9,999 for up to 10 users on Business Premium, or we can look at the Professional tier at R5,999 if your team is 10-15 people. The most cost-effective approach depends on your actual user count — let me work out what's best for your business."

### "Why are there user limits now?"

**Response:**
"Great question. Microsoft 365 licences are our largest cost component. By being upfront about what's included, we can offer a transparent, sustainable price. You only pay for the users you actually have, which means smaller businesses aren't subsidising larger ones. It's fairer and clearer."

### "Competitor X offers unlimited users"

**Response:**
"Be careful with 'unlimited' claims — they usually mean no Microsoft 365 licences at all, or they're including only Business Basic (email only, no desktop apps). Our Professional tier includes 10 full Business Standard licences with desktop Office apps, plus you can add more users at R329 each. Calculate your total cost with them including Microsoft 365 and compare apples-to-apples."

## FAQs

**Q: What if a customer already has Microsoft 365?**
A: We can either migrate their existing licences to our CSP (often at better rates) or discount the tier price by the licence value. Consult Product team for specific pricing.

**Q: Can we offer month-to-month on all tiers?**
A: Yes, all tiers are month-to-month with no lock-in. This is a key differentiator.

**Q: What's the minimum contract?**
A: No minimum contract, but free installation requires 12+ month commitment.

**Q: How fast can we activate?**
A: 3 business days from signed order (subject to SkyFibre coverage).
