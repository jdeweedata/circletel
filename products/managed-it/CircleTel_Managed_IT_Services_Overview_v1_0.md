## Executive Summary

CircleTel's Managed IT Services portfolio is strategically designed to capture the underserved SME market in South Africa, valued at approximately R10 billion. The key differentiator is the integrated approach combining connectivity and IT services under a single provider with a single bill.

By leveraging existing connectivity infrastructure, partnership with Link-up ICT/Absolute IT, and Microsoft CSP Indirect Reseller status, CircleTel is positioned to deliver comprehensive IT solutions with significant cost savings for clients.

---

## Key Differentiators

CircleTel's Managed IT Services stand apart through several competitive advantages:

- **Integrated Connectivity + IT Services** – Single provider, single bill simplifies vendor management
- **Microsoft Certified Team** – Azure Administrator (AZ-104) and Azure Virtual Desktop Specialty (AZ-140) credentials
- **3-Day Service Activation** – Rapid deployment leveraging existing infrastructure
- **Flexible Month-to-Month Contracts** – No lock-in periods, reducing client risk
- **Direct WhatsApp Support** – No call centre queues, direct access to technicians
- **Competitive Bundled Pricing** – 30-40% savings versus engaging separate providers
