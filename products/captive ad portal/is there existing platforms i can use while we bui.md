# is there existing platforms i can use while we building our own platform?

Yes, there are several strong platforms you can plug into right now — ranging from SA-specific solutions to white-label global platforms that you can brand as your own while your custom build is underway.

## 🇿🇦 SA-First Options (Start Here)

**Powerlynx** is the most relevant for your context — it's a cloud-based hotspot management and monetisation platform built specifically for ISPs and WISPs in Africa [^1]. It supports:

- Ad-based monetisation on the captive portal [^1]
- Native **1Voucher integration** for cash/unbanked users — critical for township deployments [^1]
- **DPO Pay** integration for broader African payment coverage [^2]
- No-code captive portal editor, multi-location management, user data collection, and retargeting [^1]

It has a **21-day free trial** with full unrestricted access, making it ideal to test before you commit [^1].

**Mesh Telecom** is a SA-based operator that already deploys WiFi monetisation in taxi ranks, retail malls, train stations, and stadiums — you could explore a white-label or revenue-share partnership rather than building from scratch [^3].

## 🌍 White-Label Global Platforms

These let you brand the entire experience under your own name, so you're building customer equity even before your own platform is ready:


| Platform | Key Strength | Best For |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **MyWiFi Networks** | Full white-label dashboard + portal, cloud-hosted | Agency/reseller model, multi-venue [^4][^5] |
| **Linkyfi (AVSystem)** | Advanced analytics, segmentation, ad targeting | Enterprise venues, data-driven campaigns [^6] |
| **Aislelabs** | Retail/mall focus, white-label for MSPs, sponsor/ad integration | Shopping centres, airports [^7] |
| **Spotipo** | Works with UniFi/Meraki/30+ routers, voucher system | UniFi-heavy deployments [^8] |
| **Alepo** | Telecom-grade, sponsored access + ad targeting | ISP/carrier scale [^9] |

## How to Bridge to Your Own Platform

The smart play is to use **MyWiFi Networks or Powerlynx as your white-label interim** — collect real data on ad performance, user behaviour, and venue economics now. This gives you live product intelligence (what ad formats work, what login flows convert best, what data advertisers actually want) that feeds directly into your own platform's feature roadmap.

When you're ready to migrate, you'll have real customer contracts, real data, and a validated business model — rather than building blind. Powerlynx even allows API access and webhook-based data export, so you can start pulling data into your own infrastructure early [^1].
