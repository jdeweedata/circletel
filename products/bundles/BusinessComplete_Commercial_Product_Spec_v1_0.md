## 1. Executive Summary

CircleTel Business Complete is a bundled connectivity solution for SMEs combining SkyFibre fixed wireless broadband with Arlan MTN mobile voice and 5G backup services. The product addresses the core SME need for reliable business connectivity with automatic failover and integrated voice services.

The bundle leverages CircleTel's existing SkyFibre SMB product line with Arlan MTN reseller products, creating a compelling single-vendor solution that simplifies procurement and support for businesses with 5-50 employees.

### 1.1 Strategic Metrics Summary

| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Strategic Priority | ★★★ | Primary SME offering |
| Target Market | SME 5-50 staff | Professional services, retail, light industrial |
| Base Margin | 27-30% | Before Arlan commissions |
| Technology | FWB + 5G/LTE | Tarana G1 + MTN mobile |
| Coverage | 6M+ homes/businesses | MTN FWB footprint |
| Pricing Model | Tiered + Modular | 3 tiers + add-on modules |

## 10. Installation & Provisioning

### 10.1 Site Requirements

- Clear line of sight to Tarana base station
- Signal strength ≥ -75 dBm
- Power outlet within 30m of installation point
- Roof or external wall mounting access
