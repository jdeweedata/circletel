# Venue+
## One vendor for your venue — WiFi, IoT, and support

---

### The Problem

Commercial venues juggle multiple vendors: one for WiFi, another for POS connectivity, another for sensors. Different bills, different support numbers, nobody takes responsibility when things break.

### The Solution

**Venue+** bundles managed WiFi-as-a-Service with IoT SIMs for your POS, sensors, and devices. We own the hardware, manage the network, and you get one number to call.
