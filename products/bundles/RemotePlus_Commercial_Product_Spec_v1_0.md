## 1. Executive Summary

CircleTel Remote+ is designed for the growing remote work market — freelancers, consultants, and micro-businesses (1-5 people) who need reliable home office connectivity with automatic failover to ensure they never drop a client call.

The product bundles WorkConnect SOHO connectivity with Arlan MTN LTE/5G backup services, creating a compelling work-from-home solution that competitors cannot match at comparable prices.

### 1.1 Strategic Metrics Summary

| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
