## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

BizFibreConnect™ represents CircleTel's strategic entry into the business fibre market, leveraging DFA's wholesale Business Broadband infrastructure across South Africa. This product line delivers enterprise-grade connectivity with guaranteed SLAs, positioning CircleTel competitively in the growing SME and corporate connectivity market.

### Key Highlights:
- **4 Speed Tiers:** 25/50/100/200 Mbps symmetrical (10 Mbps tier retired — effective 01 April 2026)
- **Pricing:** R1,899 – R4,373 per month (all-inclusive)
- **Coverage:** All major metros via DFA network
- **Installation:** 5–10 business days
- **Target Margin:** 25–39% across all tiers (margins compressed vs FY26 — pricing review recommended)
- **Key Advantage:** Direct DFA wholesale relationship with bundled GNNI port

> ⚠️ **MARGIN ALERT:** FY27 DFA wholesale cost increases (effective 01 April 2026) compress gross margins by 6–10 percentage points at current retail pricing. A retail pricing review is strongly recommended before the new rate card takes effect.
