## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive wholesale pricing for DFA (Dark Fibre Africa) services relevant to CircleTel's product portfolio. All prices are exclusive of VAT and effective from **01 April 2026** unless otherwise stated.

### Key Changes from FY26 (April 2025) to FY27 (April 2026)

**Business Broadband:**
- 10 Mbps speed tier **retired** — no longer available for new orders
- Contract terms expanded from 12/24-month to **1–5 year** terms
- MRC increases across all speed tiers (approx. 5–16% depending on term and speed)
- GNNI port fees: 1Gbps MRC increased from R898 → R950; NRC from R6,050 → R6,150
- 10Gbps port: NRC from R6,050 → R6,150; MRC from R3,300 → R3,350

**Magellan EPL/EVPL:**
- Contract terms expanded to **1–5 year** structure (previously 1, 2, 3, 5 year)
- New 4-year term introduced
- E-Access 1Gbps NRC: **R0 for 4-year and 5-year** contracts (previously R0 only on 5-year)
- New speed tiers: 30 Mbps, 150 Mbps, 300 Mbps, 2 Gbps, 3 Gbps, 100 Gbps
- ENNI port fees revised: 1Gbps MRC R898 → R950; 100Gbps MRC R15,000 → R10,000
- New EVPL additional VLAN pricing introduced
- Configuration changes: R1,650 → R1,683
- SFP (1Gbps Fibre Port): R1,500 → R1,530

## Cost Optimisation Strategies

### 1. Term Commitments
- **3-year contracts** now available on Business Broadband — saves 8–9% vs 1-year
- **4–5 year Magellan** contracts waive 1Gbps E-Access NRC entirely
- **5-year Business Broadband** saves up to 20% vs 1-year terms

### 2. Volume Discounts
- Helios: Move from Tier 1 to Tier 2 saves ~5%
- Aggregate services for better pricing

### 3. Product Mix
- Use Peregrine for core, Helios for last-mile
- Combine Tachyon colocation with local connectivity
- Mix managed services with dark fibre

### 4. Strategic Planning
- Plan network growth to maximise volume tiers
- Consider longer terms for stable routes
- Use burst capability instead of fixed bandwidth
- **New:** Leverage 4-year Magellan term for R0 NRC without full 5-year commitment
