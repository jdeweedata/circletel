## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive commercial schedules for DFA (Dark Fibre Africa) wholesale services, including both non-recurring charges (NRC) and recurring monthly charges (MRC). All prices are exclusive of VAT and valid from April 2025 unless otherwise stated.

### Key Pricing Categories
- **Non-Recurring Charges (NRC)**: Once-off installation, setup, and connection fees
- **Monthly Recurring Charges (MRC)**: Ongoing monthly service fees
- **Volume Discounts**: Available based on commitment levels
- **Contract Terms**: 1 to 15 years with term-based pricing

## Appendix A: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **NRC** | Non-Recurring Charge. A one-time fee for installation, setup, or activation of a service. |
| **MRC** | Monthly Recurring Charge. The ongoing monthly fee for the service. |
| **MSC** | Minimum Spend Commitment. The minimum monthly amount the customer agrees to spend. |
| **CPI** | Consumer Price Index. Used for annual price adjustments. |
| **SLA** | Service Level Agreement. Defines the performance standards and remedies for service failures. |
| **MTTR** | Mean Time To Restore. The target time to repair a service fault (Standard: 4 hours). |
| **NNI** | Network-to-Network Interface. The point where the DFA network connects to the Customer's network. |
| **GNNI** | Gigabit Network-to-Network Interface. |
| **BNG** | Broadband Network Gateway. |
| **CGNAT** | Carrier-Grade Network Address Translation. |
