## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive commercial schedules for DFA (Dark Fibre Africa) wholesale services, including both non-recurring charges (NRC) and recurring monthly charges (MRC). All prices are exclusive of VAT. Business Broadband and Magellan pricing is effective from **01 April 2026** (FY27 rate card). All other product pricing is effective from April 2025 unless otherwise stated.

### Key Pricing Categories
- **Non-Recurring Charges (NRC)**: Once-off installation, setup, and connection fees
- **Monthly Recurring Charges (MRC)**: Ongoing monthly service fees
- **Volume Discounts**: Available based on commitment levels
- **Contract Terms**: 1 to 15 years with term-based pricing

## Cost Optimisation Strategies

### 1. Term Commitments
- 3–5 year Business Broadband contracts save 8–20% vs 1-year
- 4–5 year Magellan contracts waive 1Gbps E-Access NRC (R0)
- 10–15 year dark fibre contracts save up to 80%

### 2. Volume Discounts
- Helios: Tier 2 volume saves ~5%
- Aggregate services for better pricing across products

### 3. Product Mix
- Use Peregrine for core, Helios for last-mile
- Combine Tachyon colocation with local connectivity
- Mix managed services with dark fibre

### 4. Strategic Planning
- Plan network growth to maximise volume tiers
- Consider longer terms for stable routes
- Use Calypte burst capability instead of fixed bandwidth
- Leverage new 4-year Magellan term for R0 NRC
