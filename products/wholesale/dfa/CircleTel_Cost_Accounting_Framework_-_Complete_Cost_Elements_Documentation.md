This document provides a comprehensive overview of all cost elements for cost accounting across CircleTel's complete product and service portfolio. It covers direct costs, indirect costs, installation expenses, maintenance requirements, and support structures for all technology platforms including MTN Wholesale (Tarana G1), DFA fibre services, and ECHO SP infrastructure.
