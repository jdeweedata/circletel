## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the MTN reseller products available through the partnership between CircleTel SA (Pty) Ltd and Arlan Communications (Pty) Ltd T/A MTN Ballito. The agreement, dated 29 September 2025, establishes CircleTel as a non-exclusive reseller of MTN telecommunications products.

| **Commission Protection** | Guaranteed rates for initial 3-month term |

## 1. Commission Framework

### 1.1 Base Commission Structure

CircleTel receives 30% of all commissions that Arlan Communications receives from MTN on sales generated through the reseller agreement. This commission is calculated on net subscription fees, which exclude handset fees, itemised billing, once-off charges, and VAT.

### 1.2 MTN Commission Rate Structure (Tiered)

| Monthly Subscription Range | MTN Commission Rate | CircleTel Share (30%) | Contract Term |
|---------------------------|-------------------|---------------------|---------------|
| R0 – R99.99 | 4.75% | 1.425% | 24 months |
| R100 – R199.99 | 5.75% | 1.725% | 24 months |
| R200 – R299.99 | 7.25% | 2.175% | 24 months |
| R300 – R499.99 | 8.75% | 2.625% | 24 months |
| R500 – R999.99 | 9.75% | 2.925% | 24 months |
| R1,000 – R1,999.99 | 11.75% | 3.525% | 24 months |
| R2,000+ | 13.75% | 4.125% | 24 months |

### 1.3 Commission Calculation Formula

Commissions are calculated on total contract value, not monthly recurring charges:

```
Total Contract Value = Monthly Subscription × Contract Term (months)
MTN Commission = Total Contract Value × MTN Commission Rate
CircleTel Commission = MTN Commission × 30%
```

## 4. Lifetime Value and Renewals

### 4.1 Renewal Commission Structure

CircleTel continues to receive 30% commission on all customer renewals, upgrades, and migrations without time limitation. This means commission entitlement continues beyond the initial 24-month contract and applies to all subsequent renewals and package upgrades.

### 4.2 Customer Attribution

Customers remain attributed to CircleTel for commission purposes regardless of renewal cycles. Even if a customer renews directly with MTN/Arlan, CircleTel still receives the commission.

## 5. Payment Terms and Reconciliation

### 5.1 Payment Schedule

| Event | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Sales Made | Throughout month |
| MTN Payment to Arlan | By 23rd of following month |
| Arlan Payment to CircleTel | Within 24 hours (by 25th) |
| Commission Calculation | Monthly |

### 5.2 Reporting Requirements

Arlan Communications must provide:

1. **Monthly Sales Reports** – By 5th of each month (new activations, customer details, package types)
2. **Customer Activation Confirmations** – As they occur
3. **Commission Reconciliation Statements** – By 15th of each month

### 5.3 Audit Rights

CircleTel may audit commission calculations quarterly with 7 days' notice. This includes access to commission calculation records, verification of MTN payments to Arlan, and validation of customer attributions.

---

## 6. Commission Protection Provisions

### 6.1 Rate Guarantee

Current commission rates are guaranteed for the initial 3-month term, providing stability during initial business establishment.

### 6.2 Change Requirements

Any changes to commission rates require 90 days' written notice and mutual agreement (not unilateral).

### 6.3 Viability Protection

If MTN reduces commissions, parties must negotiate in good faith to maintain commercial viability for both parties.

### 6.4 Non-Circumvention

The agreement includes a 24-month non-circumvention clause with damages for breach, including lost profits.

## 7. VAT Treatment

### 7.1 Commission VAT Handling

| Component | Treatment |
|-----------|-----------|
| Base Commission | Excludes VAT |
| Payment Received | Includes VAT (15%) |
| VAT Calculation | Base × 1.15 |

### 7.2 Example

```
Base Commission: R1,000
VAT (15%): R150
Total Payment: R1,150
```

CircleTel must issue tax invoices and pay VAT to SARS bi-monthly.

## 10. Strategic Recommendations

### 10.1 Maximising Commission Revenue

1. **Focus on High-Value Tiers** – Prioritise R1,000+ deals (11.75–13.75% MTN commission)
2. **Leverage Lifetime Value** – Build strong customer relationships for retention
3. **Volume Strategy** – Target 5–10% market share of available deals

### 10.2 Operational Excellence

1. **Commission Tracking** – Implement robust tracking via Supabase database
2. **Customer Attribution Protection** – Document all customer introductions
3. **Payment Monitoring** – Track 25th of month payment schedule

### 10.3 Future Optimisations

1. **Extend Initial Term** – Negotiate 12–24 month rate guarantee at renewal
2. **Performance Incentives** – Negotiate tiered commission for volume milestones
3. **Minimum Commission Guarantee** – Floor commission regardless of MTN rate changes
