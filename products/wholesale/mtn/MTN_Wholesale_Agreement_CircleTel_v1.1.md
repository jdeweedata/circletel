## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete MTN Wholesale product portfolio available to CircleTel (Pty) Ltd, including all product lines, associated wholesale costs, infrastructure requirements, and the Minimum Spend Commitment (MSC) schedule. The agreement covers two primary technology platforms:

1. **Fixed Wireless Broadband (FWB)** — Tarana G1 Technology
2. **Fibre-to-the-Home (FTTH)** — MTN Wholesale Fibre Infrastructure

**Note:** MTN Business LTE and 5G solutions are provided through the Arlan Communications reseller agreement and are not part of this MTN Wholesale agreement.

## 2. Product Line 1: Fixed Wireless Broadband (Tarana G1)

### 2.1 Service Overview

MTN Wholesale Fixed Wireless Broadband (FWB) service delivers high-speed, reliable internet access through MTN's national wireless Tarana network. ### 4.2 MSC Billing Rules

**From Month 4 onwards:**
- If actual billing is **less than** the MSC, the MSC amount is due and payable irrespective of actual billing
- If actual billing is **more than** the MSC, the actual billed amount is due and payable
