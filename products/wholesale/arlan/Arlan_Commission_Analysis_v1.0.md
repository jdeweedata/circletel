## Executive Summary

CircleTel has a reseller agreement with Arlan Communications (PTY) LTD T/A MTN Ballito that enables us to sell MTN telecommunications products and earn commission. This document details the products available, cost structures, commission rates, and markup opportunities.

### Key Commercial Terms

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **CircleTel Commission Share** | 30% of MTN commissions |
| **Commission Basis** | Net subscription fees (excl. devices, VAT, once-offs) |
| **Standard Contract Term** | 24 months |
| **Payment Schedule** | By 25th of each month |
| **Lifetime Value** | Commissions continue on renewals indefinitely |
| **Markup Opportunity** | CircleTel can sell above Arlan base price |

## 1. Commission Tier Structure

MTN's commission structure is tiered based on monthly subscription value. CircleTel receives 30% of all commissions paid by MTN to Arlan.

| Monthly Subscription Range | MTN Commission Rate | CircleTel Share (30%) | Contract Term |
|---------------------------|--------------------|-----------------------|---------------|
| R0 – R99.99 | 4.75% | 1.425% | 24 months |
| R100 – R199.99 | 5.75% | 1.725% | 24 months |
| R200 – R299.99 | 7.25% | 2.175% | 24 months |
| R300 – R499.99 | 8.75% | 2.625% | 24 months |
| R500 – R999.99 | 9.75% | 2.925% | 24 months |
| R1,000 – R1,999.99 | 11.75% | 3.525% | 24 months |
| R2,000+ | 13.75% | 4.125% | 24 months |

### Commission Calculation Formula

```
Total Contract Value = Monthly Subscription × Contract Term (months)
MTN Commission = Total Contract Value × MTN Commission Rate
CircleTel Commission = MTN Commission × 30%
```

## 3. Markup Opportunity Analysis

### 3.1 Markup Rules

**Critical Point:** CircleTel can sell at ANY price above Arlan's base price, as long as it remains market competitive.

- **Markup revenue is 100% CircleTel's** — not shared with Arlan
- **Commission is calculated on the RETAIL price** you charge the customer
- **Must remain competitive** with MTN direct, Vodacom, Telkom, and other dealers
- **No maximum markup** specified in the agreement

### 3.2 Recommended Markup by Product Category

| Product Category | Arlan Base Range | Recommended Markup | CircleTel Retail Range | Rationale |
|-----------------|------------------|-------------------|------------------------|-----------|
| **IoT/M2M SIMs** | R49 – R249 | 15% – 25% | R56 – R311 | Price-insensitive B2B market |
| **Fleet Management** | R99 – R299 | 15% – 20% | R114 – R359 | Value-added service bundling |
| **Business Data SIMs** | R149 – R649 | 10% – 15% | R164 – R747 | Moderate competition |
| **SMB Voice Bundles** | R300 – R599 | 10% – 15% | R330 – R689 | Service differentiation |
| **LTE/5G Routers** | R499 – R999 | 10% – 15% | R549 – R1,149 | Installation & support value |
| **Smartphone Deals** | R449 – R1,299 | 5% – 10% | R472 – R1,429 | Highly competitive market |
| **Enterprise Voice+Data** | R1,000 – R1,999 | 5% – 8% | R1,050 – R2,159 | Relationship-based sales |
| **Corporate Solutions** | R2,000+ | 3% – 5% | R2,060+ | Volume discounts expected |

### 3.3 Market Competitive Benchmarks

To ensure pricing remains competitive, benchmark against:

| Competitor | Typical Pricing vs MTN | Notes |
|------------|----------------------|-------|
| **MTN Direct** | Base price | No discount available |
| **Vodacom** | ±5% variance | Similar commission structure |
| **Telkom Mobile** | 5–10% cheaper | Less coverage, different value |
| **Cell C** | 10–15% cheaper | Network quality concerns |
| **Other MTN Dealers** | Same base as Arlan | Compete on service, not price |

**Recommendation:** Price 5–10% above competitors when bundling with CircleTel connectivity services for added value proposition.

## 6. Commission Payment & Reconciliation

### 6.1 Payment Timeline

| Event | Timeline | Reference |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Sales made | Throughout month | — |
| MTN pays Arlan | By 23rd of following month | Clause 6.3 |
| Arlan pays CircleTel | Within 24 hours (by 25th) | Clause 6.3 |

### 6.2 Monthly Reconciliation Checklist

**By 5th of Month:**
- [ ] Receive monthly sales report from Arlan
- [ ] Verify all CircleTel sales are included
- [ ] Check customer activation confirmations

**By 15th of Month:**
- [ ] Receive commission reconciliation statement
- [ ] Verify commission calculations against internal records
- [ ] Identify any discrepancies
- [ ] Submit queries within 7-day dispute window

**By 25th of Month:**
- [ ] Receive commission payment
- [ ] Verify payment amount matches statement
- [ ] Bank statement reconciliation

### 6.3 Audit Rights

Per Clause 4.3:
- CircleTel may audit commission calculations **quarterly**
- **7 days' notice** required
- Access to all commission calculation records
- Verification of MTN payments to Arlan

## 7. Key Agreement Terms

### 7.1 Commission Inclusions & Exclusions

**Commissions ARE calculated on:**
- Monthly subscription fees
- Recurring service charges
- Data bundles
- Voice bundles
- Value-added services subscriptions

**Commissions are NOT calculated on:**
- Handset/device fees
- Itemised billing charges
- Once-off activation fees
- SIM card charges
- VAT (15%)

### 7.2 Lifetime Value Protection

Per Clause 3.1:
> "CircleTel shall continue to receive 30% commission on all Customer renewals, upgrades and migrations **without time limitation**."

This means:
- Commission continues beyond initial 24-month contract
- Applies to contract renewals
- Applies to package upgrades
- Applies to service migrations
- **No expiry date** on commission entitlement

### 7.3 Customer Protection

**Non-Circumvention (Clause 8.1):**

During the agreement term and for **24 months thereafter**, Arlan shall NOT:
1. Contact, solicit or transact directly with CircleTel customers
2. Bypass CircleTel to deal directly with customers
3. Disclose CircleTel customer information to third parties
4. Attempt to divert business from CircleTel

**Customer Data Ownership (Clause 7.3):**
- All customer data belongs **exclusively** to CircleTel
- Arlan cannot use customer data for own purposes
- POPIA compliance responsibility on CircleTel
