## 1. Executive Summary

Connected Office is CircleTel's high-value anchor bundle in the Arlan/MTN reseller channel, targeting SMEs with 3–10 staff who require both fixed office connectivity and mobile devices under a single business account.

The bundle packages one office connectivity anchor (Shesha fixed-speed LTE/5G plan with free CPE) and two device upgrades (smartphones on 36-month MTN Business contracts) into a single CircleTel commercial offering. Customers are presented with one monthly cost, one account manager, and one support number — eliminating the fragmentation of managing separate contracts with MTN Business directly.

### Strategic Rationale

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Channel position** | High-value anchor in the Arlan reseller portfolio |

## 9. Fair Usage Policy

### 9.1 Shesha Plans — Fixed Speed with Data Allocation

All Shesha plans operate on a fixed-speed profile with a monthly data allocation. The FUP applies as follows:

| Phase | Data Consumed | Speed |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| Peak usage | 0–100GB (Starter) / 0–300GB (Standard) | Full contracted speed |
| Bonus data (off-peak) | Available 00:00–06:00 | Full contracted speed |
| After peak allocation exhausted (business hours) | >100GB / >300GB | Throttled to 1–4Mbps |
| Combined peak + bonus allocation exhausted | Full allocation used | Throttled to 1–4Mbps |

**Customer communication**: CircleTel will notify customers at 80% and 100% of peak data allocation via WhatsApp. Customers can request a mid-month top-up (billed at current MTN data add-on rates).

### 9.2 5G FWA 500GB Plan

500GB monthly allocation on best-effort 5G. Once 500GB is reached, speed is throttled to 1–5Mbps for the remainder of the billing month. No off-peak bonus data on FWA plans.

### 9.3 Device MFB Plans

MFB data allocations (2.3GB/6.5GB/23GB) are consumed by the SIM in the device. Once the monthly allocation is exhausted, data reverts to standard MTN rates or may be throttled depending on the specific MFB contract terms. Customers are advised to connect devices to the office Wi-Fi (via the Tozed CPE) where possible to reduce SIM data consumption.

## 10. Installation & Provisioning

### 10.1 Zero-Hardware-Cost Delivery Model

Connected Office requires no on-site technical installation. The CPE is a plug-and-play device delivered by courier.

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Arlan order submitted by CircleTel (3 line items) | Day 0 (after FICA complete) |
| 2 | MTN SIM activation and account creation | 2–5 business days |
| 3 | Tozed ZLT X100 Pro 5G CPE couriered to customer premises | 3–7 business days |
| 4 | Device smartphones couriered to customer | 3–7 business days |
| 5 | CircleTel activation call — CPE setup assistance | On delivery day |
| 6 | First invoice issued by CircleTel | Following billing cycle |

### 10.2 CPE Self-Installation

The Tozed ZLT X100 Pro 5G is designed for self-installation:
1. Insert MTN SIM (pre-activated by Arlan/MTN)
2. Connect power adapter
3. Connect to Ethernet or Wi-Fi using credentials on device label
4. Optimal placement: near window; elevated position; outdoors-capable if strong signal needed

CircleTel provides a setup guide via WhatsApp on delivery day. For complex installations (multi-floor offices, weak indoor signal), customers may request a site visit at a quoted rate.

### 10.3 Coverage Verification (Mandatory Pre-Sale)

Before issuing a Connected Office quote, CircleTel must confirm MTN indoor/outdoor signal at the customer premises:

- MTN coverage map check (mtn.co.za/business/coverage)
- If marginal coverage: request customer to test a MTN SIM on-site before signing
- Shesha and FWA plans require consistent indoor signal for reliable fixed-speed performance
