## Deal Priority Rules

1. **R1,000-R1,499 device upgrades** — sweet spot (597 deals @ R153/mo)
2. **R500-R999 data SIMs** — highest markup ratio, no device dependency
3. **R1,500+ premium devices** — whale deals @ R205/mo
4. **Voice as add-on only** — never sell standalone

**Avoid** sub-R500 deals standalone (R46/mo doesn't cover sales effort).

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| < 30 deals/month velocity | Focus device upgrades only — broadest appeal |
| Commission payment delays | 1-month cash buffer; Arlan invoice tracking in admin |
| MSC shortfall months 3-5 | Accelerate Unjani-area Tarana (existing NNI) |
| Sales bandwidth | Prioritize highest-revenue deals; defer fleet until Month 5+ |
| Customer goes direct to MTN | Value-add: single bill, bundled internet, business support |
