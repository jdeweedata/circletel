## 1. Executive Summary

Data & Connectivity is CircleTel's standalone fixed wireless and data SIM product line, sold
independently as direct office internet and mobile worker data solutions. It is sourced entirely
through the Arlan reseller channel at zero CAPEX, with CPE delivered free on qualifying plans.

**Strategic role in the portfolio:**

- **Phase 2 entry (Month 2)**: Standalone data plans activate from Month 2, generating
  immediate commission and markup revenue before the Tarana FWB infrastructure is ready
- **Connected Office anchor (Month 3)**: Shesha and Uncapped plans become the connectivity
  backbone for the Connected Office bundle (office connectivity + device upgrades)
- **Backup connectivity play**: Businesses with existing fibre or ADSL can add a 5G FWA or
  Shesha plan as a failover without replacing their primary service

**Why this product now:**

- Many South African SMEs remain on slow ADSL or do not have fibre coverage — 5G FWA and
  Shesha fixed-speed plans offer an accessible alternative with free CPE
- The free Tozed ZLT X100 Pro 5G CPE (included on Shesha and FWA plans) removes the hardware
  cost objection that blocks SMME adoption of business-grade LTE/5G
- CircleTel's single-account, WhatsApp-first model is faster than navigating the MTN Business
  portal and offers bundling potential the portal does not

**Month 2 target**: 20 standalone data plans active.
**Month 4 target**: 80 standalone plans active; Shesha plans bundled into Connected Office.

## 6. Network and Technical

### 6.1 MTN 4G/5G LTE Network

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Coverage | MTN national 4G LTE; 5G available in Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria and selected metros |
| Signal requirement | Indoor MTN 4G/5G signal confirmed before sale of Shesha or FWA plans |
| Uptime | MTN Business: 99.5% monthly network availability (network level) |
| Latency | Typical 15–50ms on 5G; 30–80ms on 4G LTE |

### 6.2 Plan Speed Profiles

| Plan Type | Speed Mechanism | Post-FUP Behaviour |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Shesha Fixed-Speed | Hard speed cap at 10/20Mbps | Throttled at 1Mbps after data allocation |
| Uncapped 5G | Best-effort up to 35/60Mbps | Throttled after 500GB/800GB FUP |
| 5G FWA | Fixed wireless allocation 500GB | Throttled after 500GB |
| Business Uncapped Wireless | Fixed speed 50/100Mbps | Fair use policy; throttled at high contention |
| Mobile Data SIMs | 4G/5G LTE; up to 100Mbps | Throttled or zero-rated after allocation |

### 6.3 Outdoor Mounting for FWA

For the 5G FWA 500GB plan in particular, outdoor mounting of the Tozed CPE significantly
improves signal and throughput. CircleTel recommends customers with marginal indoor signal
consider outdoor mounting. This is customer-arranged; CircleTel does not provide installation
for Tozed CPE. The ZYXEL ICT delivery (Uncapped Wireless plans) includes professional
installation.

## 8. Fair Usage Policy

### 8.1 FUP Disclosure [MANDATORY]

**All "Uncapped" and "Business Uncapped Wireless" plans are subject to a Fair Usage Policy (FUP).
"Uncapped" does not mean unlimited speed or data at all times. This must be disclosed to
customers in writing before signing.**

| Plan | FUP Threshold | Post-FUP Speed |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| MTN Business Uncapped 5G 35Mbps | 500GB/month | Throttled to approximately 1–5Mbps |
| MTN Business Uncapped 5G 60Mbps | 800GB/month | Throttled to approximately 1–5Mbps |
| Business Uncapped Wireless 50Mbps | FUP at high contention | Speed reduced during peak hours |
| Business Uncapped Wireless 100Mbps | FUP at high contention | Speed reduced during peak hours |

### 8.2 Shesha Data Allocation

| Plan | Primary Allocation | Bonus Allocation | Total |
|------|-------------------|-----------------|-------|
| Shesha 10Mbps | 100GB (24-hour) | 100GB (midnight–5am) | 200GB total |
| Shesha 20Mbps | 300GB (24-hour) | 300GB (midnight–5am) | 600GB total |

Night-time bonus data is only accessible during the specified hours. Customers who rely on
daytime bulk data transfers should be guided to Uncapped 5G or Uncapped Wireless plans.
