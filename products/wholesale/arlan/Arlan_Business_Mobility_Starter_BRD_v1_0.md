## 1. Purpose & Scope

This Business Rules Document (BRD) defines the operational and commercial rules that govern every Business Mobility Starter deal sold by CircleTel. It is the definitive reference for CircleTel staff and the Managing Director when qualifying customers, configuring deals, submitting orders to Arlan, and managing the customer lifecycle.

**In scope**: All Arlan/MTN device upgrade deals sold under the Business Mobility Starter product tier — single or multi-line, any device in the R500–R999/month subscription band, 36-month contracts.

**Out of scope**: Connected Office bundles (separate BRD), WorkConnect+Mobile cross-sells (see WorkConnect BRD), Fleet Connect IoT deals (separate BRD).

**Governing commercial relationship**: CircleTel ← Arlan Communications (Pty) Ltd T/A MTN Ballito ← MTN South Africa. CircleTel is the customer-facing entity. Arlan is the Master Dealer. MTN is the network operator.

## 2. Customer Eligibility Rules

### BR-ELIG-001: Business Entities Only

**Rule**: Business Mobility Starter may only be sold to formally registered business entities. Natural persons (private individuals) are not eligible.

Acceptable business registration types:
- Private company (Pty) Ltd — CIPC registration required
- Close Corporation (CC) — CIPC registration required
- Sole Proprietor — Must have a business trading name registered with CIPC or a valid tax invoice/proof of business activity
- Non-profit organisation (NPO) — Valid NPO registration document required
- Partnership — Partnership agreement or signed letter of authority required

**Reason**: Arlan/MTN Business deals are on the MTN Business tariff family. These rates are restricted to business subscribers. Consumer subscriptions are a different product category.

### BR-ELIG-002: Authorised Signatory Required

**Rule**: The person signing the deal must be a director, member, owner, or a person with a signed letter of authority from such a person. A junior employee cannot bind the business to a 36-month contract.

**Verification**: Check CIPC records match the signatory's name and ID number. If a letter of authority is used, it must be on company letterhead, signed by a director, and not more than 30 days old.

### BR-ELIG-003: Valid South African ID

**Rule**: The signatory must hold a valid South African ID document (green barcoded ID or smart card ID). Foreign nationals with a valid SA work permit may be accepted if Arlan's FICA policy permits — confirm with Arlan before quoting.

### BR-ELIG-004: FICA Compliance

**Rule**: No order may be submitted to Arlan until CircleTel has collected and verified the required FICA documentation. See Section 5 for the complete FICA document list.

**Rule**: CircleTel must retain copies of all FICA documents for a minimum of 5 years per the Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FIC Act) requirements.

### BR-ELIG-005: Credit Considerations

**Rule**: CircleTel is not a credit provider. MTN/Arlan performs its own credit assessment. However, CircleTel should not knowingly submit orders for businesses with a history of payment default. If a prospective customer mentions a previous MTN blacklisting, terminate the sales process.

## 3. Geographic Coverage Rules

### BR-GEO-001: National Coverage — No Geographic Restriction

**Rule**: Business Mobility Starter may be sold to any business in South Africa that falls within MTN's 4G or 5G commercial network coverage. CircleTel does not impose any geographic restriction.

This is a deliberate advantage over CircleTel's Tarana FWB products, which are limited to areas served by CircleTel's NNI infrastructure. Business Mobility Starter has no such constraint.

### BR-GEO-002: Coverage Pre-Qualification

**Rule**: Before quoting, confirm the customer's primary usage location has adequate MTN signal. Use the MTN coverage checker (www.mtn.co.za/coverage) as a reference. Ask the customer: "Do you currently use MTN on a personal phone in your office and on the road? Is the signal generally good?"

If the customer reports poor MTN signal at their primary location, offer the Oppo A5 Pro 4G (better antenna performance in low-signal areas) or note the limitation before proceeding.

### BR-GEO-003: Device Delivery Address

**Rule**: MTN will deliver devices to the address registered in the Arlan order. This must be a physical business address in South Africa. PO Boxes are not accepted.

## 4. Product Selection Rules

### BR-PROD-001: Target Subscription Band — R500 to R999

**Rule**: Business Mobility Starter is optimised for deals with a monthly MTN subscription of R500 to R999. This band generates R22–R29/month in commission plus R40–R100/month in markup, for a combined yield of approximately R62–R129/deal/month.

**Exception for sub-R500 deals**: Deals below R500/month (e.g. Vivo Y31 5G at R455) may only be sold when:
(a) The customer is taking 2 or more lines simultaneously, so the bundle yield exceeds R100/month total, OR
(b) The deal is a loss-leader entry point with a documented upsell plan (e.g. WorkConnect internet).

Never sell a single sub-R500 deal in isolation as a standalone transaction. The economics do not justify the sales effort.

### BR-PROD-002: Minimum Markup — 8%

**Rule**: CircleTel must apply a minimum 8% markup on every deal. This is the Arlan category floor for device upgrade deals per CircleTel's margin guardrail policy (see CT-CPS-ARLAN-BMS-2026-001 Section 4).

**Example**: If Arlan base price is R500/month, CircleTel's invoice to the customer must be at minimum R540/month.

**Rule**: Never price below the Arlan base price. Doing so creates a loss on the deal and violates the Arlan dealer agreement.

### BR-PROD-003: Target Markup — 10% Standard, 12–15% Where Market Accepts

**Rule**: The standard markup for quoting is 10% above Arlan base. If the customer is price-sensitive, the minimum is 8%. If the customer is value-led (larger business, premium device), aim for 12–15%.

### BR-PROD-004: Price Before Quoting — Always Verify Against Current Promo Sheet

**Rule**: Deal prices change with each monthly Arlan promo sheet. Never quote a customer from memory or from a promo sheet older than 30 days. Always verify the current Arlan promo sheet pricing before generating a customer quote.

If the current month's promo sheet has not been received from Arlan, request it explicitly before quoting any deal.

### BR-PROD-005: Device Availability

**Rule**: Check with Arlan that the chosen device is in stock before presenting it to a customer. Do not quote a device that Arlan confirms is out of stock. Offer the next closest alternative in the same price band.

### BR-PROD-006: One Deal Per SIM

**Rule**: Each individual SIM/line on the customer account is a separate deal. Multi-line customers have multiple individual deal submissions to Arlan, each with its own order number, SIM, and device. Do not attempt to combine multiple lines into a single Arlan order unless Arlan explicitly supports a bulk order format.

## 5. FICA & Onboarding Rules

### BR-FICA-001: Mandatory FICA Document Set

**Rule**: No order may be submitted to Arlan without the following documents:

| # | Document | Requirement |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Certified copy of director/owner South African ID | Not older than 3 months from certification date |
| 2 | CIPC Company Registration document (CoR14, CoR14.3, or equivalent) | Must be current; confirm registration not deregistered |
| 3 | Proof of business address | Utility bill, lease agreement, or municipal rates notice — not older than 3 months |
| 4 | Bank account confirmation letter | On bank letterhead; showing account number and entity name |
| 5 | Completed Arlan/MTN subscriber application form | Signed by authorised signatory |

For Sole Proprietors: replace items 2 and 4 with a business banking statement (last 3 months) showing trading activity.

### BR-FICA-002: Certified vs Uncertified Copies

**Rule**: ID documents must be certified by a commissioner of oaths (police station, notary, or bank). Uncertified copies are not accepted by Arlan/MTN and will delay provisioning.

### BR-FICA-003: Document Storage

**Rule**: CircleTel must store all FICA documents securely for 5 years. Store digitally in the customer record in the CircleTel admin portal under the customer account. Do not store FICA documents in email alone.

### BR-FICA-004: FICA Failure

**Rule**: If a customer cannot provide complete FICA documents within 5 business days of agreeing to proceed, suspend the order. Do not submit incomplete documentation to Arlan. Reactivate the order when complete documents are received.

## 6. Contract Term Rules

### BR-TERM-001: 36-Month Standard Term for Device Upgrade Deals

**Rule**: All Business Mobility Starter device upgrade deals are 36-month fixed-term contracts. This matches MTN's standard device upgrade contract term and is non-negotiable at the Arlan/MTN level.

CircleTel does not offer month-to-month terms on device deals. A 36-month contract subsidises the cost of the device supplied to the customer; month-to-month terms are not available from Arlan on this product category.

**Rule**: When presenting the contract term to customers, state clearly: "This is a 36-month fixed-term agreement. You're committed for three years. Early cancellation incurs the remaining contract value at MTN standard rates."

### BR-TERM-002: Renewal

**Rule**: At month 33 (3 months before contract end), CircleTel must proactively contact the customer to initiate a renewal discussion. At renewal, the customer selects a new device deal from the current promo sheet, which resets the 36-month clock. Commission continues on the renewed contract.

**Rule**: The renewal contact date must be logged in the CircleTel admin portal when the initial contract is activated.

### BR-TERM-003: No MTM Exceptions Without MD Approval

**Rule**: No month-to-month device deal may be offered to a customer without explicit written approval from the MD. MTM is not available through Arlan on device categories; any MTM configuration would require a non-device data SIM deal, which is a different product.

## 7. Pricing & Markup Rules

### BR-PRICE-001: CircleTel Issues Its Own Invoice

**Rule**: CircleTel invoices the customer directly for the full subscription amount (Arlan base + markup). The customer pays CircleTel. CircleTel separately receives commission from Arlan by the 25th of each month. These are two independent cash flows.

**Rule**: The customer never receives an MTN or Arlan invoice. CircleTel is the sole billing counterparty.

### BR-PRICE-002: No Discounting Below 8% Markup

**Rule**: The minimum markup is 8% above the Arlan base price. This cannot be waived without MD written approval and cannot be reduced below 0% (i.e. never below Arlan base) under any circumstances.

**Reason**: The 8% minimum markup on device deals is set by the margin guardrail policy to ensure that combined commission + markup income covers the cost of sales effort. Below 8%, the deal becomes economically marginal.

### BR-PRICE-003: VAT Inclusion

**Rule**: All customer-facing prices must be presented inclusive of VAT at 15%. The Arlan base prices are inclusive of VAT. CircleTel's markup is applied to the VAT-inclusive price, and the customer invoice is also VAT-inclusive. CircleTel must be VAT-registered to issue compliant tax invoices.

### BR-PRICE-004: No Retroactive Price Changes

**Rule**: Once a customer has accepted a quote and signed the subscriber form, the monthly subscription amount is fixed for the contract term. CircleTel may not retrospectively increase the customer's subscription amount (except where a specific MTN mid-contract price escalation clause applies, which must be disclosed at point of sale).

### BR-PRICE-005: Promotional Pricing Disclosure

**Rule**: If a deal is from a promotional promo sheet with a time-limited price, the customer must be informed that the promotional rate is subject to change on renewal. The subscription amount for the current 36-month term will not change; the renewal price may differ.

## 8. Billing & Collections Rules

### BR-BILL-001: Monthly Invoicing

**Rule**: CircleTel raises a monthly tax invoice for each active deal on the 1st of each month (or the contract anniversary date). The invoice covers the full subscription for that month.

### BR-BILL-002: Payment Terms

**Rule**: Invoices are due within 5 business days of issue. CircleTel's preferred collection method is debit order. Where a customer insists on EFT, payment is due by the 5th of the month. Accounts not settled by the 10th are in breach; refer to cancellation rules.

### BR-BILL-003: Commission Reconciliation

**Rule**: By the 28th of each month, reconcile Arlan's commission payment (received by 25th) against the number of active deals in that month. Any discrepancy of more than 5% must be raised with Arlan in writing within 3 business days.

**Rule**: Log every commission receipt in the CircleTel admin portal against the relevant Arlan deal record.

### BR-BILL-004: Customer Default

**Rule**: If a customer is 20 days overdue on payment, suspend further orders for that customer. If 45 days overdue, escalate to MD. Cancellation of the MTN contract does not release CircleTel from commission obligations to Arlan; manage collections carefully.

## 9. Cancellation & Early Termination Rules

### BR-CANCEL-001: CPA Section 14 — 20-Day Consumer Notice Right

**Rule**: The Consumer Protection Act (CPA) Section 14 grants customers the right to cancel a fixed-term contract with 20 business days' written notice. Upon receipt of such notice, CircleTel must:

1. Acknowledge receipt in writing within 2 business days.
2. Notify Arlan of the pending cancellation within 3 business days.
3. Advise the customer of the early termination fee (see below).
4. Process the cancellation via Arlan once the 20-business-day notice period expires.

### BR-CANCEL-002: Early Termination Fee

**Rule**: MTN applies an early termination fee equal to the remaining monthly subscriptions on the contract, calculated at the standard MTN rate (not the CircleTel markup rate). This fee is levied by MTN via Arlan and passed to CircleTel to collect from the customer.

**Example**: A customer cancels a R700/month plan at month 12 of a 36-month contract. Remaining = 24 months × R700 = R16,800 early termination fee (excluding VAT, as per MTN standard terms).

**Rule**: This fee must be disclosed prominently in the quote and subscriber agreement. Never omit the early termination fee from the sales conversation.

### BR-CANCEL-003: Device Return

**Rule**: Devices supplied under device upgrade deals are the property of MTN/Arlan for the contract term. On early cancellation, MTN/Arlan may require device return. CircleTel must facilitate this. Advise customers of this risk at point of sale.

### BR-CANCEL-004: Commission Clawback Risk

**Rule**: If a contract is cancelled within the first 12 months, Arlan/MTN may apply a commission clawback against CircleTel. Monitor early cancellations closely. A customer cancelling in the first 6 months will result in a clawback of some or all commission paid for that deal. Pre-qualify customers carefully to minimise early cancellation risk.

## 10. Ordering Workflow Rules

### BR-ORDER-001: Complete Workflow — No Shortcuts

**Rule**: Every Business Mobility Starter deal must follow this exact workflow. No step may be skipped.

| Step | Action | Owner | Gate |
|------|--------|-------|------|
| 1 | Qualify customer (business entity, MTN coverage, budget confirmed) | CircleTel Sales | BR-ELIG-001 to BR-ELIG-005 |
| 2 | Confirm current promo sheet pricing | CircleTel Sales | BR-PROD-004 |
| 3 | Select deal (device + plan) from current promo sheet | CircleTel Sales + Customer | BR-PROD-001, BR-PROD-005 |
| 4 | Generate customer quote (incl. markup, VAT, 36-mo term, early termination fee disclosure) | CircleTel Sales | BR-PRICE-001 to BR-PRICE-005 |
| 5 | Customer accepts quote in writing (email or WhatsApp confirmation) | Customer | Contract gate |
| 6 | Collect complete FICA documents | Customer → CircleTel | BR-FICA-001 |
| 7 | Verify FICA documents (certified ID, CIPC match, address, bank account) | CircleTel | BR-FICA-002 |
| 8 | Store FICA documents in admin portal | CircleTel | BR-FICA-003 |
| 9 | Submit order to Arlan (completed subscriber form + FICA docs) | CircleTel → Arlan | BR-ORDER-002 |
| 10 | Receive Arlan order confirmation and reference number | Arlan → CircleTel | Log in admin |
| 11 | Monitor MTN provisioning (2–5 business days) | CircleTel | Notify customer of progress |
| 12 | Confirm device dispatch and delivery tracking | CircleTel | Follow up with Arlan if > 7 days |
| 13 | Confirm SIM activation with customer | CircleTel + Customer | Activation milestone |
| 14 | Raise first customer invoice | CircleTel | BR-BILL-001 |
| 15 | Set renewal reminder (month 33) in admin portal | CircleTel | BR-TERM-002 |

### BR-ORDER-002: Arlan Submission Requirements

**Rule**: The Arlan order submission package must include:
- Completed MTN Business subscriber application form (Arlan standard form)
- All FICA documents listed in BR-FICA-001
- Deal reference from current promo sheet (device name, plan code, monthly subscription)
- Customer delivery address for device
- Customer contact number and email for SIM activation SMS/confirmation

**Rule**: Do not submit orders verbally. All orders must be submitted in writing to the designated Arlan sales contact.

### BR-ORDER-003: Order Tracking

**Rule**: Every submitted order must be logged in the CircleTel admin portal with:
- Customer name and business registration number
- Deal reference (device + plan + subscription amount)
- Date submitted to Arlan
- Arlan order reference number
- Expected activation date
- Renewal reminder date (month 33)

## 11. Prohibited Configurations

The following configurations are not permitted under Business Mobility Starter:

| Prohibited | Reason | Alternative |
|------------|--------|-------------|
| Standalone voice-only deals | Voice deals (R94/mo avg) generate less than R10/mo commission; below break-even on sales effort | Offer voice as part of a device deal |
| Single SIM data deal below R350/month | Economics do not justify sales effort | Upsell to R500+ plan or decline |
| Single device deal below R500/month to a single-line customer | R46/mo yield is below sales effort cost | Require 2+ lines or upsell to R500+ deal |
| Month-to-month contract on device upgrades | Not available from Arlan on this product category | Explain 36-month requirement; offer if suitable |
| Pricing below Arlan base (markup < 0%) | Guaranteed loss on deal | Apply minimum 8% markup always |
| Selling to consumers (individuals) | Not eligible for MTN Business tariff | Redirect to MTN consumer channels |
| Committing to device delivery date without Arlan confirmation | Creates expectation CircleTel cannot guarantee | "MTN aims for 3–7 days; we'll track it for you" |
| Stacking multiple promotions on same line | Not available in Arlan/MTN deal structure | One promotional deal per SIM per period |

## 13. Commission & Revenue Rules

### BR-REV-001: Commission Entitlement

**Rule**: CircleTel is entitled to 30% of the MTN commission paid to Arlan on each active deal. Commission accrues monthly for the lifetime of the deal, including renewals. Arlan pays by the 25th of each month for the prior month's active deals.

### BR-REV-002: Commission Rate Bands

**Rule**: Apply the following MTN commission tiers when estimating deal yield. These are indicative; verify against Arlan's current commission schedule.

| MTN Monthly Subscription | MTN Commission Rate | CircleTel Share (30%) | CircleTel Earns/mo |
|--------------------------|--------------------|-----------------------|-------------------|
| R300–R499 | 8.75% | 2.625% | R10–R13 |
| R500–R999 | 9.75% | 2.925% | R15–R29 |
| R1,000–R1,999 | 11.75% | 3.525% | R35–R70 |

### BR-REV-003: Markup Accounting

**Rule**: The markup component (difference between CircleTel's invoice to customer and Arlan's base price) is 100% CircleTel revenue. It is not shared with Arlan. Record markup revenue separately from commission revenue in the CircleTel admin portal.

### BR-REV-004: No Deal Submission Without Revenue Viability Check

**Rule**: Before submitting any deal to Arlan, verify that the combined commission + markup income for that deal meets the minimum R50/month threshold. Below R50/month, the deal does not cover CircleTel's administrative cost.

| Deal Type | Minimum Monthly Revenue to Proceed |
|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| Single line | R50/month (commission + markup combined) |
| Multi-line (2+) | R40/month per line (total bundle > R80/month) |

## 14. Escalation Rules

### BR-ESC-001: When to Escalate to Arlan

Escalate to Arlan (in writing, via the designated Arlan sales contact) in these situations:

| Situation | Action | Timeframe |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Order submitted > 5 business days with no Arlan confirmation | Follow up with Arlan | Day 6 after submission |
| SIM not activated > 10 business days after Arlan confirmation | Escalate to Arlan operations | Day 11 |
| Device not delivered > 10 business days after activation | Escalate to Arlan logistics | Day 11 |
| Commission not received by 28th of month | Query Arlan in writing | 28th of month |
| Commission discrepancy > 5% of expected amount | Formal written query with deal list | Within 3 days of receipt |
| Customer reports network fault not resolved within 5 days | Escalate to Arlan for MTN network operations | Day 6 of reported fault |

### BR-ESC-002: When to Escalate to MD

Escalate to the Managing Director in these situations:

| Situation | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| Customer requests cancellation within first 6 months | High clawback risk — MD decision required |
| Customer disputes the 36-month term (claims they were not informed) | Legal/compliance risk |
| Commission clawback notice from Arlan | Financial impact — MD oversight required |
| Arlan requests changes to dealer agreement terms | Contractual change — MD must review |
| Customer is 45+ days overdue | Collections escalation — MD decision |
| Any deal above R1,500/month subscription | High-value deal — MD awareness required |
