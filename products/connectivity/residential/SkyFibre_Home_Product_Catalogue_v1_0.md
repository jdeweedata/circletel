## 1. Portfolio Overview

**Brand:** SkyFibre Home  
**Segment:** Residential Fixed Wireless Access (FWA)  
**Technology:** MTN Tarana G1 — Licensed Spectrum  
**Coverage:** 6 million homes via the MTN network  
**Go-to-Market Readiness:** 9/10 — Ready for immediate market launch  

SkyFibre Home delivers fibre-like wireless broadband to South African households using MTN's advanced Tarana G1 platform. The portfolio comprises three speed tiers with uncapped data, 4:1 contention ratio, and month-to-month flexibility — no contracts required.

### Key Differentiators

- High-speed downloads with 4:1 contention (download/upload)
- No line-of-sight required
- Weather-resilient technology
- Load-shedding resilient
- 3–5 day installation turnaround
- Self-install or professional install options

## 9. Important Notes

**Portfolio Status:** The `skyfibre_home_residential_products.json` catalogue lists all three tiers as **active**. However, the `CircleTel_Complete_Product_Portfolio_v3_0.md` flags SkyFibre Residential as being considered for **sunset** due to MTN AirFibre channel conflict at wholesale-level pricing. The conflict relates to MTN's own retail AirFibre prices matching or undercutting CircleTel's wholesale input costs. The active JSON catalogue reflects the current retail pricing strategy (R999–R1 999) which provides positive margins (37.9%–58.9%) at these price points.

**Transition Plan (if sunset proceeds):** Existing residential FWA customers would be migrated to HomeFibreConnect (FTTH) where available, or maintained on existing contracts until natural churn. No new residential FWA sales would be taken.
