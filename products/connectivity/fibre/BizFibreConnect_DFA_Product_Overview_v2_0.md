## 1. Executive Summary

BizFibreConnect™ represents CircleTel's strategic entry into the business fibre market, leveraging DFA's (Dark Fibre Africa) wholesale Business Broadband infrastructure across South Africa. This product line delivers enterprise-grade connectivity with guaranteed SLAs, positioning CircleTel competitively in the growing SME and corporate connectivity market.

### Key Highlights

- **5 Speed Tiers:** 10/25/50/100/200 Mbps symmetrical
- **Retail Pricing:** R1,699 – R4,373 per month (all-inclusive)
- **Coverage:** All major metros via DFA network
- **Installation:** 5–10 business days
- **Target Margin:** 31–41% across all tiers
- **Key Advantage:** Direct DFA wholesale relationship with bundled GNNI port
