# MTN Consumer Packages

## Overview

CircleTel now offers **19 consumer-friendly MTN packages** across three categories: 5G Uncapped, LTE Uncapped, and LTE Broadband Capped. These packages are specifically designed for home users with simplified naming, consumer-focused features, and competitive pricing.

## Pricing Strategy

### Business vs Consumer Differentiation

**Business Packages**: 10% premium pricing for enhanced support, business features, and priority service
**Consumer Packages**: Standard pricing optimized for home users

## Customer Journey Integration

### Coverage Check → Package Selection:
1. User enters address
2. System detects MTN 5G/LTE coverage
3. Show consumer packages (hide business unless B2B customer)
4. Sort by price or data
5. Highlight "Best Value" (1TB) and "Most Popular" (Standard LTE 10Mbps)

### Filtering:
- By technology: 5G vs LTE
- By price range: <R300, R300-R500, R500+
- By data: Uncapped vs Capped
- By speed: <10Mbps, 10-20Mbps, 20Mbps+
