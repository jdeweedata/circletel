### 5. HomeFibreConnect™
**Technology:** MTN FTTH Wholesale
**Target:** Residential market (competitive alternative)

#### Current Status
⚠️ **STRATEGIC PIVOT REQUIRED** - Pricing under review due to MTN Fibre market competition

#### Revised Pricing (Competitive Model)

| Package | Speed | Revised Price | vs MTN Retail |
|---------|-------|---------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | 25/25 Mbps | R449 | +R40 vs R409 |
| **Plus** | 50/50 Mbps | R649 | +R40 vs R609 |
| **Max** | 100/100 Mbps | R649 | Matches R609 |
| **Ultra** | 200/200 Mbps | R849 | +R40 vs R809 |

#### Key Challenges
- MTN Fibre retails 100/100Mbps at R609
- Original pricing (R1,599 for 100Mbps) not competitive
- Only 2 ISPs on MTN FTTH network (limited competition)
- Margins reduced to 25-35% at competitive pricing

#### Recommended Strategy
**Option A:** Aggressive residential pricing (low margin, high volume)
**Option B:** Pivot to business focus (BizFibre branding)
**Option C:** Hybrid model with value-added bundles
