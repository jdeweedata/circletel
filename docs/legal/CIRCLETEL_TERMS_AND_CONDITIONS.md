The following activities are strictly prohibited:

#### 3.3.1 Illegal Activities
- Transmission, distribution, or storage of illegal content
- Unauthorized distribution of copyrighted material
- Hacking, cracking, or unauthorized access attempts
- Distribution of malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Fraud, identity theft, or phishing
- Harassment, stalking, or threats
- Drug trafficking or illegal substance promotion

#### 3.3.2 Network Abuse
- Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
- Port scanning or network probing without authorization
- IP spoofing or forging packet headers
- Unauthorized relay of mail or proxy services
- Running open proxies, tor exit nodes, or anonymization services
- Cryptocurrency mining on shared infrastructure
- Torrent seeding or peer-to-peer file sharing that violates terms

#### 3.3.3 Spam and Unsolicited Communications
- Sending unsolicited bulk email (spam)
- Using CircleTel services to support spam operations
- Harvesting email addresses without consent
- Operating mail servers that don't comply with RFC standards
- Falsifying header information in emails

#### 3.3.4 Resource Abuse
- Excessive consumption of bandwidth, CPU, memory, or storage
- Running scripts or processes that degrade service for other customers
- Using services in a manner inconsistent with their intended purpose
- Hosting content that violates disk space or bandwidth allocations

#### 3.3.5 Content Violations
- Posting defamatory, libelous, or slanderous content
- Distributing obscene or pornographic material
- Violating intellectual property rights
- Publishing private information without consent
- Hosting phishing sites or fake login pages

### 3.4 System and Network Security

Customers must not:
1. Attempt unauthorized access to CircleTel systems or customer accounts
2. Circumvent authentication or security measures
3. Intercept or monitor data without authorization

5. Exploit vulnerabilities for any purpose

**Additional Costs:**
- Customer liable for all damages caused to CircleTel
- Legal fees and court costs

#### 3.5.3 Service Disruption

- No service credits issued for outages resulting from AUP violations

- Customer remains liable for all fees during suspension period
