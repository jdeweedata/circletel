# CircleTel Product Sales Strategy & Promotional Campaign Plan

**Date**: 22 March 2026
**Period**: April 2026 - March 2027 (12-month bootstrap)
**Target**: R893K MRR by Month 12

---

## Executive Summary

CircleTel's go-to-market strategy sequences three revenue channels to maximize cashflow while managing MSC obligations:

1. **Arlan (Months 1-12)**: Zero-CAPEX cash machine. 10 deals/week growing to 20+/week by Month 6.
2. **Tarana SMB (Months 1-12)**: Margin builder (37-46%). Free installation campaigns on 24-month contracts.
3. **DFA Enterprise (Months 6-12)**: Profit expander (53-56%). Highest margin, lowest volume.

**Residential Tarana** is kept at current pricing (6.5-14.76% margin) as a volume play for MSC coverage, with 24-month contract requirement.

## Residential Strategy: Volume Play for MSC Coverage

Residential margins (6.5-14.76%) are below the 25% floor. Decision: **keep as-is** for market entry.

**Mitigations**:
- Require 24-month contracts on all residential (locks in LTV)
- Charge 50% installation fee minimum (R450) on residential
- Focus promotional spend (free install, free months) on SMB only
- Track residential separately with documented margin exception
- Each residential customer contributes R499/mo toward MSC coverage

---

## Cashflow Acceleration Tactics

1. **Arlan same-day activation**: Zero install, commission starts immediately
2. **Annual prepay discount**: 1 month free for 12 months upfront = R14,289 day-1 cash
3. **Installation fee upfront**: Charge at sign-up, don't amortize
4. **Partner referral channel**: R250-R500 per referral via `promotions.source = 'partner'`
5. **Unjani pilot conversion**: 20 sites start paying 15 May = ~R20K instant MRR
