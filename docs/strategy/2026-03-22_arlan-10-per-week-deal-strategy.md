## How Arlan Revenue Works (Per Deal)

Every Arlan deal generates **two revenue streams**:

1. **Commission** — MTN pays Arlan, Arlan pays CircleTel 30%
2. **Markup** — CircleTel adds a percentage on top of MTN price, keeps 100%

```
Monthly Revenue Per Deal = Commission + Markup

Commission = MTN Price (incl VAT) × MTN Rate × 30%
Markup = MTN Price (excl VAT) × Markup %
```

## The Sweet Spot: R500-R1,500/mo Deals

Curated deals are filtered to R300-R2,000 range. The **optimal revenue band** is R500-R1,500 because:

- Commission jumps from 8.75% to 9.75% at R500 (another jump to 11.75% at R1,000)
- Higher deal value = higher absolute markup revenue
- SMBs commonly spend R500-R1,500 on connectivity
- Easier to close than R2,000+ deals (less procurement friction)

## Top 5 Deal Categories to Push (Ranked by Revenue × Closability)

### 1. Data Connectivity (5G/LTE Uncapped) — Push 3/week

**Why**: Every SMB needs internet. Easiest conversation opener.

| Deal Profile | MTN Price | Markup (15%) | Selling Price | Monthly Revenue |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| 5G Uncapped 50Mbps | ~R699 | R91.17 | R804 | R111.62 |
| LTE Uncapped 30Mbps | ~R499 | R65.09 | R574 | R78.19 |
| 5G Data+ 100GB | ~R599 | R78.13 | R689 | R95.55 |

**Target customer**: Any SMB currently on Telkom/Rain/Vodacom that wants better speeds or uncapped data.

**Sales pitch**: "Get uncapped 5G for your business at R804/mo — faster than your Telkom line, no installation needed, active today."

**Monthly MRR from 3/week**: 12 deals × R95 avg = **R1,140/mo**

### 2. Backup Connectivity (LTE Failover) — Push 2/week

**Why**: Cross-sell to ANY existing connectivity customer. "What happens when your line goes down?"

| Deal Profile | MTN Price | Markup (15%) | Selling Price | Monthly Revenue |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| LTE Backup 50GB | ~R399 | R52.04 | R459 | R62.51 |

**Monthly MRR from 2/week**: 8 deals × R95 avg = **R760/mo**

### 3. IoT/M2M SIM Only — Push 2/week

**Why**: Highest markup (20%), recurring forever, businesses buy in bulk (CCTV, alarms, tracking).

| Deal Profile | MTN Price | Markup (20%) | Selling Price | Monthly Revenue |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| IoT SIM 1GB | ~R99 | R17.22 | R114 | R18.63 |
| IoT SIM 5GB | ~R199 | R34.61 | R229 | R38.04 |
| IoT SIM 10GB | ~R349 | R60.70 | R402 | R69.86 |

**BUT** — IoT deals are lower value per deal. The play is **volume per customer**: a security company might need 20-50 SIMs.

**Target customer**: Security companies (Fidelity, Chubb resellers), fleet operators, point-of-sale merchants.

**Sales pitch**: "We do IoT SIMs in bulk for security cameras and alarm systems. R114/mo per SIM, 1GB each. You've got 30 cameras? That's one invoice from CircleTel instead of managing 30 SIM cards yourself."

**Monthly MRR from 2/week (but often bulk)**: 8 deals avg 3 SIMs each = 24 SIMs × R42 avg = **R1,008/mo**

## Deals to AVOID (Low Revenue, Hard to Close)

| Category | Why Avoid |
|----------|-----------|
| Sub-R300 SIM-only | Revenue too low (~R30/deal/mo), not worth sales effort |
| Month-to-month contracts | No commission commitment, high churn risk |
| Device-only deals with no data | Low markup (8%), no recurring commission |
| Out-of-stock devices | Can't deliver, wastes pipeline time |
| Expired promotional deals | Price plan may change, creates confusion |

The curation system already filters these out (R300 minimum, 24/36-month contracts, Available/CTB devices only).

## Sales Enablement Priorities

### Week 1-2: Build the Pipeline

1. **Identify 50 target businesses** in Unjani area and surrounding business parks
2. **Categorize by deal type**: Who needs internet? Who needs backup? Who has fleets?
3. **Prepare Arlan one-pagers** per category (Data, Backup, IoT, Fleet, Device)
4. **Set up WhatsApp Business catalog** with top 10 deals per category

### Week 3-4: Close and Compound

1. **Close 10/week target** — prioritize Data Connectivity (easiest first win)
2. **Cross-sell backup** to every data connectivity customer closed
3. **Ask for referrals** on every closed deal (R500 credit program)
4. **Track weekly MRR** against execution plan Month 1 target (R6,800 Arlan MRR)

### Month 2+: Bulk and Bundle

1. **IoT bulk deals** — 1 security company = 20+ SIMs = 1 deal worth 20
2. **Fleet bulk** — 1 logistics company = 10+ SIMs
3. **Bundle with Tarana** — "Get your SkyFibre line + LTE backup + staff phones, one bill"
4. **Partner referrals** — incentivize Tarana installers to pitch Arlan add-ons

**Reality check**: 10/week gets you to ~R5,500/mo new MRR, compounding to ~R16K by Month 3. The execution plan targets R40,800 Arlan MRR by Month 3, implying **30 deals/week** or much higher deal values. This means:

1. **Bulk IoT/Fleet deals are critical** — a single security company deal with 30 SIMs counts as 30 deals
2. **Average deal value must be pushed up** — target R500+ MTN price deals only
3. **Cross-sell aggressively** — every customer gets offered backup connectivity
4. **By Month 3, you need a dedicated Arlan sales person** or partner channel
