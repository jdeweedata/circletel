# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

South African consumers and businesses seeking reliable internet connectivity (Fibre, LTE, 5G). Partners are entrepreneurs, side-hustlers, and sales-driven individuals looking to earn commission by referring customers. Users are often price-conscious but value quality local support over overseas call centres. Many access the site on mobile devices with limited data.

## Product Purpose

CircleTel provides internet connectivity solutions across South Africa with a focus on local support, transparent pricing, and no lock-in contracts. The EarnMore Partner Programme enables anyone with a network and smartphone to earn recurring commission by referring customers. Success looks like: customers who trust CircleTel for reliable service, partners who build sustainable income streams.

## Brand Personality

**Energetic, Opportunity-focused, Direct.** CircleTel speaks with confidence and urgency without desperation. The tone is action-oriented and entrepreneurial — "start earning today" not "consider our programme." No corporate jargon, no fine print gymnastics. Say what you mean, mean what you say.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS templates**: No hero-metric layouts, identical card grids, gradient text, glassmorphism, or anything that screams "AI made this." If it looks like every other startup landing page, it's wrong.
- **Big telco corporate**: Not Vodacom/MTN's busy, promo-heavy aesthetic. No overwhelming banners, asterisk-laden disclaimers, or visual noise that erodes trust.
- **Cheap/discount vibes**: No red SALE banners, starbursts, or desperate urgency. Quality over bargain-bin energy. CircleTel competes on value, not desperation.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the opportunity, don't oversell it** — Lead with what partners/customers gain. Let the value proposition speak without hype. Real numbers, real outcomes.
2. **Local beats global** — Every design choice should feel South African: language, imagery, cultural references. This isn't a Silicon Valley import.
3. **Mobile-first, data-light** — Most users are on smartphones with limited data. Every kilobyte must earn its place. Progressive loading, optimized assets, no decorative bloat.
4. **Action over explanation** — Prioritize CTAs and clear next steps over lengthy explanations. Users want to do, not read.
5. **Confidence without arrogance** — Bold claims backed by substance. CircleTel is proud of what it offers but never condescending.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.1 AA baseline** with enhanced consideration for low-bandwidth users
- Progressive image loading and lazy-loading for non-critical assets
- Core functionality must work on 3G connections within 3 seconds
- Text-based content prioritized over heavy media
- Reduced motion supported for users who prefer it
