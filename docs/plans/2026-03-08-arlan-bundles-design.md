## 7. Arlan Commission Optimization

### Commission Tiers (CircleTel receives 30%)

| Monthly Range | MTN Rate | CircleTel Share |
|---------------|----------|-----------------|
| R300-R499 | 8.75% | 2.625% |
| R500-R999 | 9.75% | 2.925% |
| R1,000-R1,999 | 11.75% | 3.525% |
| R2,000+ | 13.75% | 4.125% |

### Bundle Impact on Commission Tiers

| Bundle | Arlan Component | Commission Tier |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| SME Essential | R449 | R300-R499 (2.6%) |
| SME Professional | R948 | R500-R999 (2.9%) |
| SME Enterprise | R1,748 | R1,000-R1,999 (3.5%) |
| SOHO Starter | R149 | R100-R199 (1.7%) |
| SOHO Plus | R449 | R300-R499 (2.6%) |
| SOHO Pro | R748 | R500-R999 (2.9%) |

**Key insight**: Bundling pushes customers into higher commission tiers.
