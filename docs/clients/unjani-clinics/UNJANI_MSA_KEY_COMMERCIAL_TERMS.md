## Pricing

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Monthly fee** | **R450 per site (excl VAT)** — flat rate regardless of technology |
| Setup fees | None for standard subsidised installs |
| Non-standard installs | Quoted separately (cherry picker, civil works, cabling >30m) |
| Payment terms | 30 days from invoice, EFT in ZAR |
| Overdue interest | 2% p.a. above prime rate |

### Escalations

| Period | Rule |
|--------|------|
| Year 1 | Price locked — no escalation |
| Year 2 | CPI capped at 6% |
| Year 3+ | Annual review based on actual costs + network improvements |

---

## Term & Termination

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Minimum term** | **24 months** (Subsidised Services) |
| Notice period | 3 calendar months |
| Auto-renewal | Indefinite period after initial term |
| Early termination fee | Remaining months x monthly fee |
| Fee waiver | If CTEL can't maintain Minimum Bandwidth for 30 consecutive days |

---

## Rollout Commitments

- All sites rolled out within **12 months**, phased
- Priority to sites with existing fibre/FWA infrastructure
- Site survey within **14 days** of nomination
- Installation **5–10 business days** from approval

---

## Service Levels & Credits

| Technology | Min Bandwidth | Target | Uptime SLA |
|------------|--------------|--------|------------|
| Fibre / FWA | 25 Mbps | 25 Mbps | **99%** |
| LTE / 5G | 10 Mbps | 20 Mbps | **95%** |

| Breach | Credit |
|--------|--------|
| Below min bandwidth for 24hrs | 10% of monthly fee |
| Complete outage >24hrs | Pro-rata daily credits |
| Monthly uptime below SLA | 5% per percentage point below SLA |

---

## Equipment & Support

- CTEL provides and maintains all CPE/routers, basic WiFi, professional installation
- 8x5 remote management included
- All equipment remains **CTEL property**

### Exclusions from R450 Rate

- Cherry picker access
- Civil works >30m
- Backup power
- Premium 24/7 support
- Equipment replacement due to negligence/theft/vandalism
