## Deal Context

These MTN Business packages were negotiated through **Arlan** (MTN wholesale/reseller channel) at volume-discounted rates specifically for the Unjani Clinics rollout. The connectivity underpins the Circle Tel–Unjani MSA (R450/site/month) and the TDX advertising monetisation partnership.

While secured for Unjani, both packages are available to sell at **normal retail pricing incl. VAT** to the broader market.

## Side-by-Side Comparison

| Feature | 5G 60 Mbps | LTE 20 Mbps |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Negotiated Price** | R579 incl. | R499 incl. |
| **Speed** | 60 Mbps | 20 Mbps |
| **FUP** | 800 GB | 600 GB |
| **Latency** | 15–25 ms | 20–30 ms |
| **Free Router** | Yes (Huawei 5G CPE) | No (SIM only or +R70–110/mo) |
| **Coverage** | Metro areas only | Nationwide |
| **Best For** | Primary link in 5G areas | Fallback or non-5G locations |

**Key trade-off**: 5G gives 3× speed, larger FUP, lower latency, and a free router — but coverage is limited to metro areas. LTE has nationwide reach and is more suitable as a fallback or for rural clinics.

## Unjani Rollout Economics

### Volume Context

- **Phase 2 sites**: 28 clinics (see `revised-projections.csv`)
- **Total target**: 50 sites across Unjani network
- **Provinces**: Primarily Eastern Cape (18 sites), Free State (6 sites), Gauteng, others

### Cost Stack per Site

| Item | Monthly |
|------|---------|
| Unjani pays Circle Tel (capped MSA fee) | R450 excl. VAT |
| Circle Tel pays MTN (5G negotiated) | R579 incl. VAT (~R503 excl.) |
| Circle Tel pays MTN (LTE negotiated) | R499 incl. VAT (~R434 excl.) |
| **Connectivity shortfall (5G)** | **~R53/site/month** |
| **Connectivity surplus (LTE)** | **~R16/site/month** |

### Recovery via TDX Advertising

The connectivity cost gap is designed to be recovered through the TDX WiFi monetisation partnership:

- **TDX Tier 1 recovery**: First R450/site/month from Net Ad Revenue → Circle Tel
- **Revenue share above recovery**: 50/50 split (CT/TDX)
- **Barcelona benchmark target**: R2,964/month gross ad revenue at optimised sites
- **Current actual average**: R184/month (pre-optimisation)

### Incumbent ISP Comparison

Many Unjani sites currently pay significantly more than the negotiated MTN rates:

| Current ISP | Typical Cost | Sites |

| Morclick | R649–R1,299 | 4 |
| Herotel | R699 | 3 |

The negotiated Arlan rates (R499–R579) undercut most incumbent ISPs, strengthening the migration business case.
