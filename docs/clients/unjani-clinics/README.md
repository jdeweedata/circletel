## About Unjani Clinics

Unjani Clinics is a non-profit organization operating a network of primary healthcare clinics across South Africa. Each clinic is operated by a registered nurse, providing essential healthcare services to underserved communities.

**Key Characteristics:**
- NPO providing affordable healthcare
- Clinics operated by registered nurses
- Focus on underserved communities
- Network of 252+ clinics across all provinces
- Head office in Midrand with regional clinics nationwide

## Special Conditions

### Subsidised Healthcare Services

This agreement includes special provisions for subsidised healthcare connectivity:

1. **Network Evolution Commitment** - CircleTel commits to upgrade services as infrastructure improves
2. **Service Credits** - Pro-rata credits for outages exceeding 24 hours
3. **Equipment** - All CPE/routers provided and maintained by CircleTel
4. **Priority Support** - Business hours phone support included

### Early Termination

- Early termination fee: Remaining months x monthly fee
- Fee waived if CircleTel cannot maintain Minimum Bandwidth for 90 consecutive days
- Equipment must be returned within 30 days of termination
