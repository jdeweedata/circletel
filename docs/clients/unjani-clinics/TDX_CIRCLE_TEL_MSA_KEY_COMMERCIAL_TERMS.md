## Commercial Context

This agreement is linked to the **Circle Tel – Unjani Clinics MSA** (R450/site/month connectivity). The R450 connectivity fee is ring-fenced for Unjani only. The upside revenue comes from **TDX's Free WiFi captive portal advertising platform** serving patients at the clinics.

**Barcelona Performance Benchmark**: R2,964/month proven ad revenue at optimised Unjani sites — this is the target for mature sites.

## Revenue Structure (Clause 7)

### Net Revenue Formula

```
Net Revenue = Gross Ad Revenue − Sales Commissions − Platform Fee (15%)
```

- **Sales Commission Cap**: Max 20% of Gross Advertising Revenue

### Tier 1: Cost Recovery

| Technology | Recovery Amount |
|------------|----------------|
| FWA / Tarana | R450/site/month (subject to 95% SLA) |
| LTE / 5G | R450/site/month **OR** 100% of Net Revenue if below R300 threshold |

### Tier 2: Revenue Share

| Recipient | Share of Net Revenue above Recovery |
|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| **Circle Tel** | **50%** |
| **TDX** | **50%** |

### Unjani NPC Payment (Clause 7.4)

Unjani NPC pays Circle Tel a separate capped **R450/site/month** for connectivity — this is the ring-fenced subsidised fee from the Unjani MSA.

### Market Volatility

Neither party liable for CPM/demand fluctuations (acknowledged in agreement).

|------|----------------------|----------|------------------------|----------------|
| **Tier 1 — High** | ≥ R450/mo | Full R450 | 50% / 50% | No limit |
| **Tier 2 — Moderate** | R200 – R449 | R150 | 55% / 45% | Max 3 consecutive months |
| **Tier 3 — Low** | R50 – R199 | None | 55% / 45% | Max 3 months + mandatory joint review |

### Bad Debt Exclusions

- Max 10% of Gross Ad Revenue in rolling 3-month period
- Excluded revenue reconciled within 7 days
- Written off after 180 days

---

## Exclusivity (Clause 4)

- TDX has **sole and exclusive right** to monetise WiFi and captive portal at the Sites
- Circle Tel must **not** allow competitive advertising
- **Default protection**: If TDX fails to pay for 2 consecutive months → TDX exclusivity suspended → Circle Tel may engage alternative ad partners. TDX must remove software within 30 days.
