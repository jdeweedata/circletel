## Executive Summary

### Key Findings

After analyzing 5 competitor partner/affiliate landing pages, several consistent patterns emerged:

**Common Patterns Across All Sites:**
1. âœ… **Clear value proposition** in hero section (above fold)
2. âœ… **Multiple conversion paths** - CTAs at 3-5 locations throughout page
3. âœ… **Visual "How It Works"** diagrams or step-by-step explanations
4. âœ… **Benefit cards** - 3-6 key benefits with icons
5. âœ… **Social proof** - Testimonials, case studies, or partner logos
6. âœ… **FAQ sections** - Address common concerns and questions
7. âœ… **Marketing resources** - Downloadable materials or tools

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Vox**: Brand consistency, multiple programme types, premium positioning
- **Openserve**: Enterprise B2B focus, product showcase, network coverage maps
- **HostAfrica**: Affiliate model, lower barrier to entry, educational content

**Best Overall Design**: Vox Channel Partners (most comprehensive, best UX)
